## Emergency Protocols

### When You Feel Like Quitting
1. Review your Day 1 assessment
2. Calculate money left on table
3. Message accountability partner
4. Create one tiny thing
5. Share it immediately

### When You're Stuck
1. Review successful pieces
2. Ask audience what they need
3. Copy a format that works
4. Teach what you just learned
5. Document the struggle

### When Nothing's Working
1. Analyze your data
2. Survey your audience
3. Study competitors
4. Pivot your approach
5. Test new platforms
