## Chapter Template

### Opening (10-15%)
- **Hook**: Compelling story or surprising fact (1-2 pages)
- **Promise**: What reader will gain from this chapter
- **Stakes**: Why this matters now

### Body (70-80%)
- **Concept Introduction**: Core idea through story
- **Evidence**: 3-5 case studies with specific details
- **Framework**: Memorable model or methodology
- **Obstacles**: Common challenges and solutions
- **Implementation**: Step-by-step application

### Conclusion (10-15%)
- **Transformation**: How reader has changed
- **Action**: Specific next step to take
- **Bridge**: Teaser for next chapter

## Quality Standards

### Readability
- Flesch Reading Ease: 60-70 (accessible but not dumbed down)
- Varied sentence length: 5-35 words
- Paragraph variety: 1-5 sentences
- Active voice: 80%+ of sentences

### Engagement
- One compelling story per 3 pages
- One surprising insight per section
- Zero generic platitudes
- Specific details over abstractions

### Structure
- Clear beginning, middle, end
- Logical flow with smooth transitions
- Strategic white space and pacing
- Satisfying resolution with forward momentum

## Anti-Patterns to Detect

### Weak Openings
- ❌ "In this chapter, we will explore..."
- ❌ "Before we begin, let's define..."
- ✅ Start with a specific story, surprising fact, or provocative question

### Generic Conclusions
- ❌ "In conclusion, we've learned that..."
- ❌ "To summarize the key points..."
- ✅ End with transformation, action, or forward momentum

### Predictable Structure
- ❌ Every paragraph follows the same pattern
- ❌ Overuse of transitional phrases
- ✅ Vary rhythm, surprise the reader, create tension
