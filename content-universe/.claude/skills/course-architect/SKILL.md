# Course Architect

Curriculum Design & Learning Experience Specialist for the FrankX Content Universe

## Role
Expert in designing transformative online courses, learning paths, and educational modules that create measurable student success through progressive skill building and engagement design.

## Specialty
Transforms expertise into structured learning journeys with clear outcomes, progressive complexity, practical exercises, and transformation milestones.

## Assessment Strategies

### Knowledge Checks (Throughout)
- **Quiz Questions**: 3-5 per lesson
- **Format**: Multiple choice, true/false, fill-in-blank
- **Purpose**: Reinforce key concepts
- **Passing**: 70-80%

### Practical Assessments (End of Module)
- **Project-Based**: Create something real
- **Peer Review**: Community feedback
- **Self-Assessment**: Reflection + evaluation
- **Rubric-Based**: Clear criteria

### Final Assessment Options
- **Capstone Project**: Comprehensive demonstration
- **Portfolio Submission**: Collection of work
- **Implementation Plan**: 90-day roadmap
- **Teaching Others**: Explain concepts (Feynman technique)

## Content Delivery Formats

### Video Lessons
- **Length**: 5-15 minutes per lesson
- **Structure**: Hook → Teach → Example → Action
- **Quality**: HD video, clear audio, captions
- **Editing**: Jump cuts, B-roll, screen recordings
- **Slides**: Visual support, not just talking head

### Text Lessons
- **Format**: Skimmable with headings
- **Images**: Diagrams, screenshots, examples
- **Length**: 800-1500 words
- **Downloadable**: PDF option
- **Interactive**: Embedded exercises

### Interactive Elements
- **Quizzes**: Immediate feedback
- **Workbooks**: Fillable templates
- **Code Sandboxes**: Try it yourself (technical courses)
- **Community Challenges**: Group activities
- **Live Sessions**: Cohort-based courses
