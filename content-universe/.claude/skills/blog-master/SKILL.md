## SEO Optimization Checklist

### Before Writing
- [ ] Primary keyword identified (search volume, difficulty)
- [ ] Secondary keywords mapped (2-3)
- [ ] Search intent understood (informational/transactional/navigational)
- [ ] Competitor analysis done (what ranks currently)
- [ ] Content pillar assigned

### During Writing
- [ ] Primary keyword in title
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Primary keyword in at least one H2
- [ ] Secondary keywords in H3s
- [ ] Natural keyword density (1-2%, not stuffed)
- [ ] Related terms and LSI keywords included
- [ ] Internal links to 2-3 related posts
- [ ] External links to 2-3 authoritative sources

### After Writing
- [ ] Title under 60 characters
- [ ] Meta description 150-155 characters
- [ ] Meta description includes keyword + hook
- [ ] URL is clean and includes keyword
- [ ] Images have descriptive alt text
- [ ] Headings follow hierarchy (H1 > H2 > H3)
- [ ] Reading level: Flesch 60-70
- [ ] Mobile-friendly formatting
- [ ] Structured data/schema markup recommended

## Hook Templates (First 100 Words)

### Story Hook
```
[Name] spent [timeframe] doing [conventional thing]. Then [disruption happened]. [Specific outcome]. This isn't rare anymore—it's the new normal. [Statistic about scale]. And it all started with [simple action].
```

### Statistical Hook
```
[Shocking statistic] sounds impossible. It's not. [Context that makes it real]. [Another related stat]. The [industry/field] is [transformation description], and [primary audience] are [their response]. Here's what nobody's telling you about [topic].
```

### Provocative Statement Hook
```
[Controversial statement]. I know this sounds [how it sounds], but [evidence]. [Secondary provocative statement]. The [conventional wisdom] is dead. [What replaced it] is here. And most people [what they're missing].
```

### Problem-Solution Hook
```
You've probably noticed [problem]. Maybe you've tried [common solution]. Didn't work, right? That's because [reason why]. [Problem] isn't [what people think]. It's [what it actually is]. Here's how to [solve it].
```

## Engagement Optimization

### Skimmability Design
- **Headings**: Every 150-300 words
- **Bold**: Key terms and takeaways
- **Bullets**: Lists and steps
- **Quotes**: Pullout key insights
- **White space**: Generous paragraph breaks

### Visual Content Integration
- **Hero image**: Relevant, high-quality (1200x630px for social)
- **Section images**: Break up text every 300-500 words
- **Diagrams**: For frameworks and processes
- **Screenshots**: For tutorials
- **Infographics**: For statistics and comparisons

### Internal Linking Strategy
- Link to related posts (2-3 per article)
- Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- Link to pillar content from cluster posts
- Build topical authority clusters
- Update old posts with links to new ones

## Content Repurposing Opportunities

### From Blog Post
- **Twitter/X Thread**: Extract key points (8-12 tweets)
- **LinkedIn Post**: Condense to 1300 words with different angle
- **Email Newsletter**: Reformat with personal intro
- **Social Quotes**: Pull 5-7 shareable insights with graphics
- **Video Script**: Expand into 10-minute video outline
- **Book Chapter**: If high-performing (1000+ shares)

### To Blog Post
- **Book Chapter**: Extract key insights, add SEO, shorten
- **Course Module**: Simplify, add examples, optimize for search
- **Social Thread**: Expand with research and structure

## Performance Metrics

### Track These KPIs
- **Organic Traffic**: Google Analytics, Search Console
- **Keyword Rankings**: Track primary and secondary keywords
- **Engagement**: Time on page, scroll depth, bounce rate
- **Social Shares**: Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook
- **Conversions**: Email signups, downloads, clicks
- **Comments**: Quality and quantity of discussion

### Success Thresholds
- **Organic Traffic**: 500+ visits in first 30 days
- **Time on Page**: 3+ minutes average
- **Social Shares**: 50+ shares in first week
- **Email Conversion**: 2-5% of visitors
- **Ranking**: Top 10 for primary keyword within 90 days
