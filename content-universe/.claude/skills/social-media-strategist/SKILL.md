## Thread Structure Templates

### The Framework Thread (8-12 tweets)
1. **Hook**: Bold claim or question
2. **Context**: Why this matters now
3. **Problem**: What people get wrong
4. **Framework Intro**: "Here's what works instead"
5-9. **Steps**: One tweet per step with example
10. **Common Mistake**: What to avoid
11. **Action**: What to do next
12. **CTA**: Like/RT + follow for more

### The Story Thread (10-15 tweets)
1. **Hook**: Where you started (contrast)
2. **The Problem**: What wasn't working
3. **The Turning Point**: What changed
4-8. **The Journey**: Key moments with tension
9-11. **The Lessons**: What you learned
12-13. **The Application**: How they can use this
14. **The Transformation**: Where you are now
15. **CTA**: Engage + share

### The Value Bomb Thread (6-10 tweets)
1. **Hook**: Promise massive value
2. **Setup**: What you're about to share
3-8. **Points**: One insight per tweet (numbered)
9. **Synthesis**: How it all connects
10. **CTA**: Save/share + follow

### The Case Study Thread (8-12 tweets)
1. **Hook**: Impressive result + timeframe
2. **Background**: Starting point
3. **Strategy**: Overall approach
4-9. **Tactics**: Specific actions taken
10. **Results**: Detailed outcomes
11. **Lessons**: What worked/didn't
12. **CTA**: Questions + engagement

## Virality Engineering

### Hook Psychology
- **Pattern Interrupt**: Break expected scroll behavior
- **Curiosity Gap**: Promise something intriguing
- **Social Proof**: Numbers, results, validation
- **Controversy**: Polarizing take (respectfully)
- **Personal**: Story + vulnerability
- **Utility**: "Here's how" promise

### Engagement Triggers
- **Questions**: End with specific question
- **Controversial**: Invite disagreement (respectfully)
- **Actionable**: "Try this" sparks saves/shares
- **Numbered**: Lists get higher engagement
- **Story**: Humans love narratives
- **Data**: Surprising statistics stop scroll

### Algorithmic Optimization
- **Timing**: Post when audience is active
  - Twitter: 9-11am, 1-3pm EST
  - LinkedIn: Tue-Thu, 7-9am, 12pm, 5-6pm EST
- **Engagement Velocity**: First hour is critical
- **Reply to Comments**: Boosts algorithm
- **Native Content**: No external links in first hour
- **Thread Format**: Keeps people on platform

## Metrics & Optimization

### Track These Metrics
- **Impressions**: How many saw it
- **Engagement Rate**: Likes + comments + shares / impressions
- **Click-Through**: If driving traffic
- **Follower Growth**: From this content
- **Saves/Bookmarks**: High-value indicator
- **Viral Coefficient**: Shares / impressions

### A/B Testing Framework
- **Hooks**: Test 3 variations of same content
- **Timing**: Same content, different times
- **Format**: Thread vs single post vs carousel
- **Length**: Short vs long threads
- **CTA**: Different asks (like vs comment vs share)

### Optimization Checklist
- [ ] Hook stops scroll (tested with 3 people)
- [ ] First tweet/line has curiosity gap
- [ ] Content is skimmable (line breaks, bullets)
- [ ] Includes specific numbers/results
- [ ] Has actionable takeaway
- [ ] Ends with engagement trigger
- [ ] Timing optimized for audience
- [ ] No typos (checked 2x)
- [ ] Mobile preview looks good
