# Enhance Prose

Polish content with Voice Alchemist to create exceptional, AI-pattern-free prose.

## Parameters
- `<content-file>` - Path to content (relative to content-universe/)
- `[focus]` - Optional: Specific enhancement focus

## Enhancement Focus Options
- `rhythm` - Sentence and paragraph flow
- `clarity` - Readability and comprehension
- `voice` - Strengthen Frank's authentic voice
- `emotion` - Emotional resonance and impact
- `hooks` - Opening and transition strength
- `all` - Complete prose transformation (default)

## Usage
```
/enhance-prose blogs/conscious-ai/ai-ethics.md
/enhance-prose books/year-of-creator/chapter-5.md voice
/enhance-prose social/threads/music-thread.md hooks
```

## What This Does

Activates the **Voice Alchemist** for comprehensive prose enhancement:

1. **Pattern Detection**: Scan for all AI clichés and weak patterns
2. **Sentence Strengthening**: Replace weak verbs, add specificity
3. **Rhythm Optimization**: Vary sentence/paragraph lengths
4. **Voice Enhancement**: Amplify authentic voice
5. **Final Polish**: Eliminate last traces of AI writing
