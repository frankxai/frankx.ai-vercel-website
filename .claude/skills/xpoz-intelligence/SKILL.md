# Xpoz Intelligence

The "what's working and why" engine. Replaces manual doomscrolling with queryable evidence.

## Monday sweep (standard queries)
1. Top-performing posts last 7d in pillars: AI architecture, agentic systems, creator OS, gym/performance crossover.
2. Hook patterns: first-line structures of top 20 posts per pillar — cluster into named patterns.
3. Competitor watch: configured handles — anything >3x their baseline engagement gets a Higgsfield video analysis (see higgsfield-operator).
4. Demand check: cross-validate long-form candidates against Semrush keyword/trend data.

## Output contract
Brief per pillar: 3 long-form candidates + 10 short hooks, each with evidence link and the pattern it instantiates. File into Notion Content Engine as `Idea` items with outlier evidence attached. NO candidate without evidence — blank-page ideation is banned.

## Distillation feed
Patterns observed 3+ times across weeks → promote to a named framework in Obsidian `Frameworks/` (starlight-memory insight-distillation). The framework name then becomes vocabulary in script briefs.

Free tier 5K credits validates the loop; upgrade to Pro ($20) only after two full weekly cycles prove signal.
