# FrankX.AI Customer Journeys

> Complete mapping of user paths through the hub

---

## Primary Personas

### 1. The Curious Creator
**Who**: Someone interested in AI music or creative AI but hasn't started yet
**Entry**: Homepage, Music Lab, Blog
**Goal**: Understand what's possible, get inspired, start creating

### 2. The Learning Builder
**Who**: Student or professional wanting to learn AI skills
**Entry**: Students page, Courses, Resources
**Goal**: Gain certifications, build skills, advance career

### 3. The Active Creator
**Who**: Already creating with AI, wants to level up
**Entry**: Prompt Library, Vibe OS, Products
**Goal**: Better workflows, professional-grade prompts, systems

### 4. The Enterprise Explorer
**Who**: Business professional curious about Frank's expertise
**Entry**: About, LinkedIn, Enterprise
**Goal**: Understand credibility, potential collaboration

## Journey 1: Creator Discovery Path

```
Homepage → Start → Music Lab → Prompt Library → Vibe OS → Newsletter
```

### Step-by-Step

| Step | Page | Purpose | CTA |
|------|------|---------|-----|
| 1 | `/` (Homepage) | First impression, understand the hub | "Pick Your Path" → /start |
| 2 | `/start` | Overview of all options | Choose: Music Lab, Learning, Prompts, or Resources |
| 3 | `/music-lab` | Understand AI music creation | "Get Vibe OS" or "Browse Prompts" |
| 4 | `/prompt-library` | Explore real prompts | Copy prompts, explore categories |
| 5 | `/products/vibe-os` | Deep dive on the system | "Get Started" or Newsletter |
| 6 | `/creation-chronicles` | Subscribe to newsletter | Email signup |

### Key Connections
- Homepage hero → /start (primary CTA)
- Homepage cards → /music-lab, /prompt-library, /students
- Music Lab → /prompt-library (for music prompts)
- Music Lab → /products/vibe-os (for the full system)
- Prompt Library → /products/vibe-os (upgrade path)
