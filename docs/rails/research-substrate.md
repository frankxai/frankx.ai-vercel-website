# Research Substrate — Contemplative Rails

> The canon every rail entry leans on. Verbatim from the original handoff §4. Treat this as the primary corpus.

## Christian mystical tradition

- Meister Eckhart — Sermons (Walshe ed.). Especially Sermon 48 ("the eye through which I see God") and the apophatic sermons.
- Teresa of Ávila — *Interior Castle*
- John of the Cross — *Dark Night of the Soul*, *Ascent of Mount Carmel*
- The Desert Fathers — *Sayings*
- Hesychast tradition — *The Philokalia*, especially the Jesus Prayer literature
- Pseudo-Dionysius — *The Mystical Theology* (apophatic source text)
- C.S. Lewis — *Mere Christianity*, *The Problem of Pain*, *Till We Have Faces*
- Dietrich Bonhoeffer — *Letters and Papers from Prison*, *The Cost of Discipleship*
- Thomas Merton — *New Seeds of Contemplation*

## Advaita / Non-dual tradition

- Ramana Maharshi — *Be As You Are* (ed. Godman, 1985), *Talks*
- Nisargadatta Maharaj — *I Am That* (1973)
- Mooji — *White Fire*, public talks
- Sesha — Spanish-language Advaita teacher, recorded talks
- Rupert Spira — *The Nature of Consciousness*, *Being Aware of Being Aware*
- Adyashanti — *The End of Your World*

## Sufism

- Rumi — *Masnavi*, *Divan-i Shams-i Tabrizi*
- Ibn Arabi — *Fusus al-Hikam*, *Meccan Revelations*
- Hafiz — *Divan*

## Kabbalah / Jewish mysticism

- Gershom Scholem — *Major Trends in Jewish Mysticism* (1941, scholarly entry point)
- *Zohar* (Pritzker edition for accessibility)
- Daniel Matt — *The Essential Kabbalah*
- Concepts: Ein Sof, Tzimtzum, Sefirot, Tikkun Olam

## Buddhism (Mahayana, Zen, Dzogchen)

- Pema Chödrön — *When Things Fall Apart*
- Stephen Levine — *A Year to Live*
- Shunryu Suzuki — *Zen Mind, Beginner's Mind*
- Thich Nhat Hanh — *The Heart of the Buddha's Teaching*
- Concepts: Buddha-nature, suchness, emptiness, dependent origination

## Neville Goddard / Imaginal tradition

- Neville Goddard — *The Power of Awareness*, *Feeling Is the Secret*, *The Law and the Promise*
- Frame: God as human imagination, *I AM* as creative consciousness

## Modern consciousness science

- David Bohm — *Wholeness and the Implicate Order* (1980), *Thought as a System* (1992)
- Roger Penrose — *The Emperor's New Mind* (1989), *Shadows of the Mind* (1994)
- Hameroff & Penrose — "Consciousness in the Universe: A Review of the Orch OR Theory" (*Physics of Life Reviews*, 2014)
- Rupert Sheldrake — *The Presence of the Past* (1988), *Science Set Free*
- David Deutsch — *The Beginning of Infinity*
- Donald Hoffman — *The Case Against Reality* (2019)
- Bernardo Kastrup — *The Idea of the World*, analytic idealism
- Iain McGilchrist — *The Master and His Emissary*, *The Matter With Things*

## Modern teachers (state, practice, embodiment)

- Joe Dispenza — *Becoming Supernatural*, *Breaking the Habit of Being Yourself*
- Eckhart Tolle — *The Power of Now*, *A New Earth*
- Michael Singer — *The Untethered Soul*, *The Surrender Experiment*
- Wim Hof — *The Wim Hof Method*

- Aldous Huxley — *The Perennial Philosophy*
- Huston Smith — *The World's Religions*, *Forgotten Truth*

- William James — *The Varieties of Religious Experience*
