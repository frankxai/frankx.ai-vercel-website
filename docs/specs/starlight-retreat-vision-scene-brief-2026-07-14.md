# Starlight Retreats — scene brief

## Creative thesis

The retreat is a working room with a horizon: a cinematic Mediterranean table where reflection becomes an artifact, then a practice that survives the return home.

## Direction board

- Keep: editorial calm, lived-in hospitality, real working materials, strong horizon, restrained amber warmth over FrankX obsidian.
- Avoid: luxury-resort advertising, yoga and spa clichés, conference staging, giant holograms, fake venue branding, generic AI nodes and card walls.
- Signature: the “room pathfinder” changes the recommended format and status from a visitor’s chosen intent.
- Static composition: one wide Tier B hero image, one dominant headline and one clear primary action.
- Motion job: local state continuity in the pathfinder and a quiet image-scale hover on the homepage teaser. No scroll choreography or WebGL is justified.
- Reduced motion: all information and state remain available without transforms or transitions.
- Mobile: image becomes a tall cinematic crop; truth strip wraps; pathfinder buttons become a two-column grid; ladder remains a vertical readable sequence.

## Hero asset contract

- Tier: B, generated-owned.
- Role: world backdrop and concrete visual explanation of “working retreat.”
- Target crop: 16:9 master with safe central subject and usable dark negative space on the left; responsive `object-position` handles mobile.
- Required caption: “Representative concept visual — not a confirmed venue.”
- Performance target: WebP or AVIF, ideally under 500 KB after optimization.
- Accessible equivalent: alt text describes a small group working around a natural table in a Mediterranean setting; no venue identification.

## Copy posture

FrankX directness with a warmer soul register. Name mechanisms, artifacts and status. No Arcanea mythology, guru promise, medical framing or guaranteed transformation.
