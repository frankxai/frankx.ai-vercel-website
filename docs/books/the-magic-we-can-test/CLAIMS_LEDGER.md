## Quantum language approved for the book

Use:

> Google's Willow results are extraordinary evidence of controlled quantum computation and error correction. Hartmut Neven interpreted the benchmark as lending credence to David Deutsch's many-worlds account. That is a serious interpretation, not an experimental proof that parallel universes exist.

Do not use:

> Google proved the multiverse, therefore every desired future already exists and consciousness can select it.

## AI language approved for the book

Use:

> Frontier models increasingly perform difficult scientific, mathematical, coding, and multimodal tasks. Their abilities are uneven, their outputs remain fallible, and the consequences of delegating judgment grow with capability.

Do not use:

> AGI has arrived, the model understands everything, or superintelligence will manifest any future we prompt.

## Promotion workflow

A claim changes badge only when:

1. a human editor reviews the underlying source;
2. at least one credible competing or limiting source is checked;
3. the wording is updated in manuscript, web, and companion prompt;
4. edition and review dates change;
5. the changelog records why.
