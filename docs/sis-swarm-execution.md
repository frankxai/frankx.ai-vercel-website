# SIS Swarm Execution Note

FrankX.ai is the public authority surface.

Starlight Intelligence Systems is the execution infrastructure behind the surface: agentic operating systems, AI CoE systems, cloud AI architecture, MCP-to-cloud workflows, and business/creator intelligence systems.

Arcanea is the creative/IP universe. It appears on FrankX as books, worldbuilding, education, and creative intelligence, not as the dominant FrankX brand voice.

## Execution Model

The platform is organized as a practical swarm:

- Product architecture: routes, offer ladder, conversion paths.
- Engineering: Next.js App Router pages, typed static registries, reusable components.
- UX/UI: dark-first, premium, high-density, responsive pages with restrained motion.
- Content architecture: research, blog, products, books, and cloud pages share one vocabulary.
- SEO and routing: metadata, route enumeration, and verified link registries.
- QA: type-check, lint, claims audit, link checks, build, and route render checks.

## Operating Rule

Research should produce artifacts:

1. Signals
2. Notes
3. Frameworks
4. Playbooks
5. Experiments
6. Products
7. Canonical systems

The site should make this loop visible without exposing private systems or confidential material.
