## What I Could Do Better (Self-Assessment)

### Current Weaknesses in My Content Generation

1. **Feature-First Tendency**: I default to explaining "what" before "why it matters to you"
2. **Missing Studio Metaphors**: I explain systems without grounding in creative practice
3. **Overusing Corporate Words**: "Leverage," "stakeholder," "game-changer" slip in
4. **Skipping Emotional Setup**: I start with definitions instead of scenes
5. **Over-Engineering**: Some articles are too long, too comprehensive

### How I Will Improve

For every article moving forward:

1. **Open with a scene** - "2 AM in the studio" energy before any definitions
2. **Use signature phrases** - Include at least one per article
3. **Check forbidden words** - Scan for "leverage," "synergy," etc. before publishing
4. **Add "In the Studio" sections** - Show real creator workflows
5. **Keep it tight** - 1,500-3,000 words unless flagship
6. **Verify statistics** - Link to CLAIMS_REGISTRY
7. **Include creator transformation promise** - "You'll go from X to Y"
