## Voice & Tone

### Do

- Direct, no fluff
- First person ("I" not "Frank")
- Practical, actionable
- Honest about what works and doesn't
- Warm but not cheesy

### Don't

- No corporate speak
- No credential dumping ("500+ projects!")
- No hype or exaggeration
- No empty motivation
- No third-person bio speak

### Example Transformations

**Instead of:**
> "With over 4 years of enterprise experience and 500+ successful implementations..."

**Write:**
> "I've spent years building AI systems for large companies. Here's what I learned..."

**Instead of:**
> "FrankX.AI is a leading platform for..."

**Write:**
> "This is where I share what I'm learning about..."
