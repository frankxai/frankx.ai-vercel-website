# /ai-architecture — cinematic hero image brief

> For local generation via **Codex (`$imagegen` / gpt-image-2)** and **Antigravity 2.0 (Nano Banana Pro)**, then side-by-side review. Grounded in `taste.md` (cinema not stock, studio-rooted, restraint) + `design.md` tokens (#0a0a0b void, emerald/cyan accents, Poppins display).

## What the image is for

The hero of `frankx.ai/ai-architecture`. Headline: **"Build the agent system you can operate."** Deck (first person): *"I've seen AI systems run at enterprise scale, and watched a lot of impressive demos fall over the week they met real traffic…"* The image must feel like **calm competence and quiet mastery** — a system you can trust under load — not hype, not a glowing brain, not a robot.

Target placement: full-bleed background behind the hero grid (kept dark enough that white Poppins headline stays legible), or a right-column film-still beside the headline. 16:9, 2K+.

## Two concepts — generate both, on both engines

### Concept A — "The Control Plane" (human presence, heartfelt)
> Cinematic film still, wide establishing shot of a darkened architect's studio at night. A lone figure sits at a minimal desk, seen from behind in silhouette, facing a large pane of dark glass onto which a calm, legible system-topology diagram glows in emerald and cyan — three connected planes reading like a control plane. Volumetric rim light separates the figure from the near-black background (#0a0a0b). Shallow depth of field, subtle anamorphic flare, fine 35mm film grain, muted teal-and-charcoal palette with one restrained emerald accent. Mood: late-night focus, quiet mastery. Photographic realism. No text overlays, no glowing brain, no robots, no stock-photo smiles, no neon clutter. 16:9.

### Concept B — "The Model" (abstract, most ownable)
> Cinematic studio render, a physical architectural model of a software system floating in a near-black void (#0a0a0b). Three translucent smoked-glass planes stacked in isometric perspective — an experience layer, a runtime layer, an intelligence layer — connected by fine light-conduits in emerald and cyan tracing a request path. Edge-lit, soft key light upper-left, deep shadow, subtle volumetric haze. Materials: smoked glass, brushed graphite, thin luminous lines. Palette: charcoal and teal, single emerald accent. Mood: precise, engineered, calm. Photoreal render, shallow depth of field, fine grain. No text, no people, no brain iconography, no circuit-board cliché, no neon. 16:9.

## Engine notes
- **Nano Banana Pro (Antigravity):** search-grounded, best at legible technical labels. If you keep the topology labels in Concept A, NB Pro will render real, correctly-spelled words — lean into that. Ask for 2K.
- **gpt-image-2 (Codex):** strongest cinematic composition + lighting. Lean into the film-still mood; expect it to abstract labels into texture (fine — don't force text on this engine).

## Review rubric (score each 0–2; ~11/14 to advance; then it still passes `@visual-brand-guidelines` + the visual-creation council before publish)
1. **Palette lock** — near-black #0a0a0b base, emerald+cyan accents only. Reject blue/purple neon or warm drift.
2. **No AI-slop iconography** — reject glowing brains, humanoid robots, holographic-UI cliché, circuit-board texture, binary rain.
3. **Cinematic, not stock** — real depth/light, film-still framing. Reject flat "person-at-laptop-smiling."
4. **Restraint** — one focal idea, negative space honored. Reject busy/cluttered.
5. **Legibility** (label version only) — any words are real and correctly spelled. Reject gibberish text.
6. **Ownable** — looks like FrankX specifically, not every AI landing page. Reject generic.
7. **Mood match** — calm competence / quiet mastery, matches "the system you can operate." Reject hype/explosive.
