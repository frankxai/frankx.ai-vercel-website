### 2. Content Sweet Spot Framework

**What It Is:**
A methodology for finding the intersection of audience interest, personal capability, and authentic authority.

**Why This Matters:**
Most creators either:
- Create what they want (no audience)
- Chase trends (no authenticity)
- Copy others (no differentiation)

The "sweet spot" is where all three align.

**The AI-Powered Process:**

1. **Audience Interest Analysis (Ask AI)**

2. **Personal Capability Mapping (Ask AI)**
   - Skills inventory
   - Experience documentation
   - Unique perspective identification

3. **Authority Audit (Ask AI)**
   - Credibility signals
   - Proof points
   - Story assets

4. **Sweet Spot Identification (Ask AI)**
   - Cross-reference all three
   - Generate content pillar recommendations
   - Create content calendar
