# FrankX ICP Hero — Scene Brief

## Outcome

The first viewport tells founders, creators, and AI leaders what FrankX helps them build before it introduces the studio's media. Music remains a living proof surface, never the public promise or mandatory entry point.

## Copy architecture

- Fixed promise: **Build what comes next.**
- Governed rotating outcomes: architect an AI operating system; build an AI Center of Excellence; create compounding agentic workflows; explore the systems behind the work; ship the next version of a business.
- Primary route: AI architecture and systems.
- Secondary route: the Mind Palace intent navigator.
- Studio release: visible after the promise on mobile and beside it on desktop, user initiated.

## Motion contract

- Runtime: Motion / `framer-motion`, because this is component-state choreography rather than a scroll timeline.
- Beat: 550ms masked vertical transition, 3.2s reading hold, one phrase at a time.
- Hydration and reduced motion: one server-stable best phrase; no timer or animated substitution until the client is hydrated, and none at all when reduced motion is preferred.
- Accessibility: a stable screen-reader headline; the visual rotation is hidden from assistive technology to prevent repeated announcements.
- Performance: transform and opacity only; no text scramble, per-letter animation, filters, or new media payload.

## Preservation contract

The Suno release, GlowCards, Mind Palace, books, long-form rooms, and existing GSAP atlas remain intact. This correction owns only the first-viewport hierarchy, language, CTA order, and rotating outcome treatment.
