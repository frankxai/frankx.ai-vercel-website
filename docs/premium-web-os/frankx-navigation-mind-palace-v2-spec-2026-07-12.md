# FrankX Navigation + Mind Palace v2

## Outcome contract

- Audience: founders, creators, AI leaders, and sophisticated operators turning AI capability into owned systems, products, and operating leverage.
- First task: understand what FrankX can help them build and reach the right system without decoding the whole estate.
- First win: open a relevant architecture, system, venture, or learning path in one decision.
- Business role: authority, qualified exploration, product discovery, and conversion into the architecture and ecosystem routes.
- Proof: every room links to a real public route or verified public venture.

## Language brief

The ICP already understands agents, workflows, operating systems, and Centers of Excellence. Copy should name those mechanisms directly.

Use: architect, build, orchestrate, owned systems, operating advantage, Center of Excellence, products, proof, inspect, ship.

Avoid: internal queue/runtime language, vague transformation promises, agency-client language, inflated empire language, and claims without inspectable evidence.

Hero promise: **Turn AI capability into an operating advantage.**

Governed changing outcomes:

1. Explore your highest-leverage AI move.
2. Architect your AI operating system.
3. Build your AI Center of Excellence.
4. Orchestrate agents around real work.
5. Ship products that compound.

Primary action: **Explore AI Architecture**. Secondary action: **Map the Ecosystem**.

## Visual direction board

Keep:

- Obsidian canvas, editorial typography, restrained emerald/cyan technical signal.
- Existing reviewed music release as optional studio proof.
- Real routes, ventures, products, and operating systems.
- Glow as focus and connection state, not default decoration.

Avoid:

- Generic constellation/orbit wallpaper.
- Another interchangeable card wall.
- Decorative 3D, fake dashboards, generated text, or stale system counts.
- Low-contrast gray on the changing outcome.
- A desktop graph compressed into a mobile viewport.

Signature:

- A code-rendered architectural blueprint. FrankX is the central command room; public ventures and systems assemble around it, and corridors reveal how the work connects.

System:

- Tier A truth: real public routes and verified live ventures.
- Tier C medium: exact code-rendered diagram, controls, labels, corridors, and accessible equivalents.
- Desktop: 3×3 spatial plan with one central room and eight connected rooms.
- Mobile: sequential room trail with the same information and no essential motion.

## Acceptance gates

- Mobile menu opens at every viewport below `lg`, traps focus, closes on Escape/route selection, restores focus, and preserves body scroll state.
- Hero outcome contrast exceeds the large-text minimum and is visually subordinate to the stable promise without reading as disabled.
- Automatic changing text has a visible pause/play control and a reduced-motion static route.
- Mind Palace works as a strong still frame before motion.
- Desktop and mobile communicate the same system relationships with different compositions.
- No pinning or scroll trap; all links remain usable before, during, and after animation.
- Production HTTP, route, runtime-error, and responsive visual checks pass.
