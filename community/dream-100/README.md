# Dream 100 - Strategic Creator & Influencer Network
**Purpose**: Track top creators, thought leaders, and potential collaborators in the FrankX ecosystem

---

## What is the Dream 100?

The **Dream 100** is a strategic relationship-building framework where you identify 100 key influencers, creators, and thought leaders in your space, then systematically build relationships through:
- Engaging with their content
- Providing value first
- Collaboration opportunities
- Cross-promotion

## Engagement Strategy

### Phase 1: Research & Mapping (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Identify 100 people across categories
- [ ] Document their platforms, audience size, content focus
- [ ] Note recent projects and interests
- [ ] Find mutual connections

### Phase 2: Value First (Week 3-8)
- [ ] Engage thoughtfully with their content (not spam)
- [ ] Share their work with your audience (genuine recommendations)
- [ ] Provide value (insights, feedback, resources)
- [ ] Build genuine relationship before asking for anything

### Phase 3: Collaboration (Month 3+)
- [ ] Pitch collaboration ideas aligned with their goals
- [ ] Make it easy (provide templates, do the work)
- [ ] Deliver exceptional value in collaborations
- [ ] Follow up and maintain relationship

---

## Tracking System

Each Dream 100 member gets tracked with:
- **Name & Handle**: Full name and primary social handle
- **Platform**: Primary platform (YouTube, X, Substack, etc.)
- **Audience Size**: Estimated reach
- **Content Focus**: What they create about
- **Engagement Status**: Not Started / Engaging / Relationship / Collaborated
- **Last Interaction**: Date and type of last touchpoint
- **Collaboration Ideas**: Specific projects to pitch
- **Notes**: Insights, mutual connections, personal info

---

## Weekly Engagement Routine

**Monday: Research (30 min)**
- Add 5 new people to Dream 100
- Update engagement status on existing relationships

**Tuesday-Thursday: Engage (15 min daily)**
- Comment thoughtfully on 5 posts
- Share 2 pieces of content with your audience
- DM 1-2 people with genuine value (no asks)

**Friday: Review (30 min)**
- Review this week's interactions
- Identify warm relationships ready for collaboration
- Draft 1-2 collaboration pitches
