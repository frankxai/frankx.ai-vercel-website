**Graduation Path:**
1. Tier 3 (Customer) → Multiple purchases or high engagement
2. Tier 4.1 (VIP) → Consistent evangelism + collaboration interest
3. Tier 5 (Inner Circle) → Apply/invite to exclusive community

**Invitation Criteria:**
- Strong values alignment (conscious tech, creator empowerment)
- Active participation mindset (not passive consumers)
- Can commit to monthly engagement
- Willing to give feedback and co-create
- Energy adds to community (not drains it)
