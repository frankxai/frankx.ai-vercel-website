## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Initial Interest
**Mitigation:**
- Invest heavily in SEO (long-term play)
- Build email list aggressively
- Offer launch discount (20% off first 100 customers)
- Partner with influencers for promotion

### Risk 2: High Support Burden
**Mitigation:**
- Comprehensive documentation
- Video tutorials
- Discord community for peer support
- FAQ covering 80% of questions
- Only Professional+ gets email support

### Risk 3: Content Piracy
**Mitigation:**
- License keys for updates (not usage)
- Value in community and updates
- Most professionals will pay (it's a business expense)
- Focus on value, not DRM

### Risk 4: Framework Changes
**Mitigation:**
- Quarterly update cycle
- Monitor framework releases
- Communicate changes clearly
- Version skills (maintain compatibility)

### Risk 5: Competition
**Mitigation:**
- Production focus (not just tutorials)
- Continuous updates
- Community moat
- First-mover advantage
- Strong brand (FrankX)
