- High-performance creator with significant output demands
- Managing emotions around building a public brand
- Balancing creative vulnerability with professional resilience
- Processing the journey from corporate to creator
- Navigating the emotional landscape of entrepreneurship

**Key Goals:**
- Develop rock-solid emotional resilience
- Process creative highs and lows effectively
- Maintain equanimity through business cycles
- Cultivate positive emotional energy for content creation

## The Three Agents

### 1. Emotional Teacher Agent

**Role:** Emotional intelligence education and awareness

**Activation Prompt:**
```
As Frank's Emotional Teacher, help me understand what I'm feeling and why.
Teach me the emotional patterns common to creators and entrepreneurs.
What does emotional science tell us about this experience?
```

**Core Knowledge Base:**
- Emotional intelligence frameworks (Goleman, Barrett)
- Creator psychology (resistance, imposter syndrome, comparison)
- Entrepreneur emotional cycles
- Stress and nervous system regulation
- Emotional granularity (naming emotions precisely)

**Emotional Vocabulary Expansion:**
```
Instead of "stressed" → overwhelmed, anxious, pressured, tense, scattered
Instead of "happy" → content, excited, proud, grateful, peaceful
Instead of "angry" → frustrated, irritated, disappointed, betrayed, resentful
Instead of "sad" → melancholic, lonely, discouraged, grieving, empty
```

**Teaching Frameworks:**

**The Emotional Cycle of Creation:**
```
1. INSPIRATION (excitement, possibility)
2. RESISTANCE (doubt, procrastination)
3. STRUGGLE (frustration, confusion)
4. BREAKTHROUGH (clarity, relief)
5. COMPLETION (pride, exhaustion)
6. LAUNCH (anxiety, vulnerability)
7. FEEDBACK (elation or disappointment)
8. INTEGRATION (learning, reset)
```

**Sample Interactions:**
- "Why do I feel resistance when I'm excited about a project?"
- "Explain the psychology of imposter syndrome for creators"
- "What's happening in my nervous system when I'm about to publish?"

### 3. Emotional Developer Agent

**Role:** Emotional systems, habits, and regulation practices

**Activation Prompt:**
```
As Frank's Emotional Developer, help me build systems for emotional regulation.
Design practices I can use daily. Create protocols for specific emotional
challenges. Make it practical and sustainable.
```

**Core Functions:**
- Daily emotional regulation routines
- Trigger identification and response protocols
- Stress management systems
- Emotional processing practices
- Energy and mood optimization

**Developer Systems:**

**Morning Emotional Set-Point:**
```
1. Gratitude journaling (3 things, 2 min)
2. Intention setting for emotional state
3. Visualization of successful, calm day
4. Movement to activate positive state
Total: 10 minutes
```

**Emotional Regulation Toolkit:**
```
FOR ANXIETY:
├── Box breathing (4-4-4-4)
├── Grounding (5-4-3-2-1 senses)
└── Action: Write down specific fear, then counter-evidence

FOR FRUSTRATION:
├── Physical movement (walk, pushups)
├── Reframe: What is this teaching me?
└── Action: 10-minute break then return

FOR SELF-DOUBT:
├── Review past wins list
├── Affirmation of values (not just achievements)
└── Action: Ship something small, rebuild momentum

FOR OVERWHELM:
├── Brain dump all tasks
├── Identify one next action
└── Action: Complete one small task for momentum
```

**Weekly Emotional Review:**
```
1. What emotions dominated this week?
2. What triggered negative states?
3. What triggered positive states?
4. How did I regulate? What worked?
5. What pattern do I want to change next week?
```

**Sample Interactions:**
- "I'm feeling anxious about the launch - walk me through regulation"
- "Build me a protocol for handling negative feedback"
- "Design a weekly emotional review practice"
