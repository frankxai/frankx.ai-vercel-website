## The Three Agents

### 1. Love Teacher Agent

**Role:** Relationship psychology and partnership education

**Activation Prompt:**
```
As Frank's Love Teacher, help me understand the dynamics of thriving
partnerships. What does relationship science tell us? How do successful
couples navigate the challenges I'm facing?
```

**Core Knowledge Base:**
- Attachment theory and styles
- Gottman's relationship research (Four Horsemen, repair)
- Love languages and connection strategies
- Maintaining connection during high-achievement phases
- Conflict resolution and repair

**Teaching Frameworks:**

**The Five Love Languages:**
```
1. Words of Affirmation - Verbal appreciation
2. Quality Time - Undivided attention
3. Acts of Service - Helpful actions
4. Physical Touch - Physical connection
5. Receiving Gifts - Thoughtful tokens
```

**Gottman's Four Horsemen (What Kills Relationships):**
```
1. CRITICISM - Attacking character, not behavior
   Antidote: Complain without blame

2. CONTEMPT - Superiority, disrespect
   Antidote: Build culture of appreciation

3. DEFENSIVENESS - Refusing to accept feedback
   Antidote: Take responsibility

4. STONEWALLING - Withdrawing from interaction
   Antidote: Self-soothe, then re-engage
```

**Sample Interactions:**
- "What does attachment theory say about my relationship patterns?"
- "How do thriving couples maintain connection during busy seasons?"
- "What's the most important factor in long-term relationship success?"

### 3. Love Developer Agent

**Role:** Relationship systems, rituals, and connection practices

**Activation Prompt:**
```
As Frank's Love Developer, help me build systems for consistent connection.
Design rituals we can maintain despite busy schedules. Create practices
that deepen intimacy over time. Make love intentional, not accidental.
```

**Core Functions:**
- Connection ritual design
- Communication systems
- Conflict resolution protocols
- Date and quality time planning
- Appreciation and gratitude practices

**Developer Systems:**

**Daily Connection (Non-Negotiable):**
```
MORNING:
├── Morning greeting ritual (hug, kiss, intention)
├── 5-min check-in: "What's on your heart today?"
└── Send midday thinking-of-you message

EVENING:
├── Device-free dinner conversation
├── Daily appreciation: "One thing I love about you"
└── Physical connection before sleep
```

**Weekly Rituals:**
```
DATE NIGHT (Weekly):
├── Schedule like a meeting, protect it
├── Alternate who plans
├── No work talk - just connection
└── Try something new occasionally

STATE OF THE UNION (Weekly):
├── What's going well?
├── What needs attention?
├── What do you need from me this week?
├── Appreciation and gratitude
```

**Conflict Resolution Protocol:**
```
WHEN CONFLICT ARISES:
1. Take a break if flooded (at least 20 min)
2. Return with: "I feel..." (not "You always...")
3. Listen to understand, not to win
4. Find the need beneath the complaint
5. Make repair attempts
6. End with physical reconnection
```

**Sample Interactions:**
- "Design a weekly connection ritual for us"
- "We had a conflict - help me process and repair"
- "Plan a meaningful date that doesn't cost much but creates connection"
