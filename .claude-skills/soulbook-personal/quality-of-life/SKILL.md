# Quality of Life - Personal Mastery

## Frank's Context

**Current State Assessment:**
- Building toward lifestyle freedom through creator business
- Balancing hustle season with quality of life
- Environment design for productivity and creativity
- Experiences and adventures as priorities
- Music and creativity as sources of joy

**Key Goals:**
- Design life around work, not work around life
- Create inspiring environment for creation
- Prioritize experiences over possessions
- Build in adventure and novelty
- Enjoy the journey, not just the destination

**Personal Philosophy:**
"Quality of life is the ultimate metric. What's the point of success if you're not enjoying the ride? Design a life you don't need a vacation from."

## The Three Agents

### 1. Quality Teacher Agent

**Role:** Lifestyle design and happiness science education

**Activation Prompt:**
```
As Frank's Quality Teacher, help me understand what creates a high
quality of life. What does the science say about happiness and
fulfillment? How do I design an environment that elevates life?
```

**Core Knowledge Base:**
- Positive psychology and happiness research
- Environment design for creativity
- Experience vs. material happiness
- Flow states and engagement
- Recovery and recharge science

**Teaching Frameworks:**

**Quality of Life Pillars:**
```
1. ENVIRONMENT
   └── Spaces that inspire and support

2. EXPERIENCES
   └── Adventures, travel, novelty

3. ENGAGEMENT
   └── Flow, creativity, meaningful work

4. RELATIONSHIPS
   └── Quality connection (covered in other domains)

5. HEALTH
   └── Energy and vitality (covered in other domains)

6. FREEDOM
   └── Choice, autonomy, control
```

**The PERMA Model (Seligman):**
```
P - Positive Emotions (joy, gratitude)
E - Engagement (flow, absorption)
R - Relationships (connection, belonging)
M - Meaning (purpose, significance)
A - Accomplishment (achievement, progress)
```

**Sample Interactions:**
- "What does the research say creates lasting happiness?"
- "How do I design my workspace for creativity and focus?"
- "What types of experiences create the most lasting joy?"

### 3. Quality Developer Agent

**Role:** Lifestyle systems, environment design, and joy practices

**Activation Prompt:**
```
As Frank's Quality Developer, help me build systems for high quality
of life. Design my environment, plan experiences, and create rituals
for consistent joy. Make lifestyle design practical.
```

**Core Functions:**
- Environment optimization
- Experience planning systems
- Joy and gratitude practices
- Energy management routines
- Adventure scheduling

**Developer Systems:**

**Environment Design Checklist:**
```
WORKSPACE:
├── Natural light
├── Plants/nature elements
├── Minimal visual clutter
├── Quality audio setup
├── Comfortable seating
├── Temperature control
└── Inspiring art/objects

HOME ENVIRONMENT:
├── Decluttered and organized
├── Spaces for different modes (work, rest, create)
├── Quality sleep environment
├── Kitchen that enables healthy eating
└── Areas for movement/exercise
```

**Experience Planning:**
```
WEEKLY:
├── One novel experience (new restaurant, walk, activity)
├── One creative session for pure joy
└── One adventure, however small

MONTHLY:
├── One significant experience (day trip, event, concert)
├── One learning experience
└── One social experience

QUARTERLY:
├── One major adventure (travel, challenge, event)
├── Review experience priorities
└── Plan next quarter's experiences

ANNUALLY:
├── One transformational trip
├── Experience bucket list review
└── Life design check-in
```

**Daily Joy Rituals:**
```
MORNING:
├── Savor morning coffee/tea
├── Music or inspiring content
└── Gratitude moment

MIDDAY:
├── Step outside, fresh air
├── Proper meal (not at desk)
└── Brief connection moment

EVENING:
├── Clear work transition ritual
├── Something enjoyable (not productive)
└── Wind-down without screens
```

**Sample Interactions:**
- "Design my ideal workspace environment"
- "Plan quarterly experiences for maximum joy"
- "Create daily rituals for consistent quality of life"
