# EpicWays Client Intelligence

## Overview

Design TheEpicWays as a human-led leadership company with a private intelligence layer behind it. Keep Estefania's voice, room energy, and trust in front; use FrankX systems backstage for preparation, memory, follow-up, outcomes, and content reuse.

## Non-Negotiables

- Do not replace her public language with a technology narrative.
- Do not default to blockchain, NFTs, smart contracts, tokens, or marketplace language unless Frank explicitly asks in that turn.
- Do not invent private biography, client names, claims, metrics, revenue, or credentials.
- Keep the public voice warm, modern, direct, and easy for her to approve.
- Make the system useful to her clients: clearer diagnosis, better rooms, stronger follow-up, and measurable behavior change.
- Use premium rounded surfaces, icon-led controls, liquid glass depth, and workflow diagrams. Hard rectangular card walls fail this surface.
- Use GSAP, Framer Motion, XYFlow, or Mermaid only when they clarify loops or make the system inspectable.

## Grounding Sources

Use public-safe source material first:

- TheEpicWays official site and approved public profiles.
- User-provided screenshots, notes, photos, or copy.
- Existing FrankX design, skill, agent, and plugin conventions.
- Any explicit approvals from Estefania or Frank.

If a source is missing, label the item as a proposed internal system, not a public claim.

## Operating Loop

Build around five loops:

1. **Signal**: capture client context, team tensions, leadership gaps, event goals, language, and constraints.
2. **Shape**: turn the signal into a clean offer: workshop, program, 1:1, event, or retainer.
3. **Room**: prepare agendas, exercises, facilitation notes, prompts, and live-room energy cues.
4. **Memory**: turn the room into summaries, decisions, next actions, manager scripts, and client records.
5. **Growth**: reuse approved insights as proposals, case studies, talks, newsletters, clips, and future programs.

## Agent Pack

Use these agents as conceptual modules, Claude/Codex skills, plugin skills, or later product agents:

- **Client Signal Agent**: prepares intake questions, reads notes, extracts tensions, and identifies the strongest workshop angle.
- **Offer Architect**: packages TheEpicWays work into clear products, tiers, outcomes, and proposal language.
- **Workshop Designer**: turns a client need into agenda, exercises, room flow, facilitation prompts, and materials.
- **Room Memory Agent**: converts session notes into decisions, commitments, owner lists, and follow-up packs.
- **Impact Analyst**: maps qualitative room change to practical measures: clarity, ownership, retention risk, decision speed, energy.
- **Content Repurposer**: transforms approved workshop themes into posts, scripts, newsletters, talks, and event assets.
- **Client Portal Steward**: keeps client deliverables organized across docs, decks, sheets, recordings, and next-step trackers.

## Offer Ladder

Prefer a simple ladder:

- **Diagnostic Session**: leadership room readout, gap map, and recommended path.
- **Intensive Workshop**: focused room for communication, ownership, alignment, or manager confidence.
- **Leadership Lab**: multi-session program with repeatable behavior change and manager tools.
- **Event / EpicVoices**: interviews, panels, curated rooms, and community-led leadership experiences.
- **Client Intelligence Retainer**: ongoing preparation, follow-up, measurement, and content support.

## Public Design Direction

Use a 2026 liquid-system direction without becoming decorative:

- Large rounded surfaces: `rounded-[2rem]`, `rounded-[2.5rem]`, and `rounded-full` CTAs.
- Layered translucent material over meaningful gradients or workflow visuals.
- Lucide icons for tasks, loops, controls, and agents.
- XYFlow or Mermaid for architecture and delivery maps.
- GSAP only for accent motion, glowing connectors, and workflow life; never animate reading text.
- Reduced-motion fallback must preserve meaning.
