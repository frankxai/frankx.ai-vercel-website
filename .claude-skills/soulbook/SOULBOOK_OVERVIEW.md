## The Three Life Books

### 📖 LIFE SYMPHONY
*"Compose Your Extraordinary Life"*

For artists, musicians, and creative souls. Uses musical metaphors.

| Movement | Pillar | Theme |
|----------|--------|-------|
| 1. Rhythm | Energy | The beat that drives you |
| 2. Melody | Mind | The tune you think in |
| 3. Harmony | Soul | The chords that align you |
| 4. Composition | Craft | The music you create |
| 5. Amplification | Capital | The reach of your sound |
| 6. Ensemble | Circle | The players in your orchestra |
| 7. Masterwork | Legacy | The symphony you leave behind |

---

### 📖 GOLDEN PATH
*"Walk Your Journey to the Golden Age"*

For seekers, visionaries, and golden age believers. Uses journey metaphors.

| Waypoint | Pillar | Theme |
|----------|--------|-------|
| 1. Vitality | Energy | Fuel for the journey |
| 2. Clarity | Mind | Seeing the path |
| 3. Alignment | Soul | Walking true |
| 4. Mastery | Craft | Skills for the road |
| 5. Abundance | Capital | Resources for the quest |
| 6. Fellowship | Circle | Companions on the path |
| 7. Arrival | Legacy | The golden destination |

---

### 📖 THE 7 PILLARS
*"Build Your Life Architecture"*

For builders, systems thinkers, and architects. Uses architectural metaphors.

| Pillar | Focus | Question |
|--------|-------|----------|
| 1. Energy | Physical foundation | "Do I have vitality for my vision?" |
| 2. Mind | Mental & emotional | "Is my mind clear and regulated?" |
| 3. Soul | Values & purpose | "Am I aligned with what matters?" |
| 4. Craft | Skills & output | "Am I mastering my work?" |
| 5. Capital | Resources & freedom | "Do I have resources for my mission?" |
| 6. Circle | Relationships | "Are my relationships thriving?" |
| 7. Legacy | Integration & impact | "Is my life architecture complete?" |
