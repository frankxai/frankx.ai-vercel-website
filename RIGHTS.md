# FrankX Rights Policy

Status: production-site policy
Last updated: 2026-06-27

This file makes the public/private/gated rights model explicit for the
production site.

## Model

- Public: pages, posts, selected demos, selected release notes, and selected
  partner proof.
- Private: source assets, partner briefs, rate cards, CRM, contracts, splits,
  legal notes, unreleased music, unreleased art, wallet keys, and private
  strategy.
- Gated: products, templates, workshops, paid downloads, inner-circle material,
  commercial license kits, and partner-only implementation packs.

## Defaults

| Surface | Default rights |
| --- | --- |
| Website source and copy | All rights reserved. |
| Brand names and logos | All rights reserved. |
| Public blog and guide content | Read-only public access; no redistribution or training use unless explicit. |
| Paid products and downloads | Product terms control usage. |
| Music and art | All rights reserved unless a release page or agreement says otherwise. |
| Open-source packages | Covered only by their package-level license. |
| Partner pages | Public proof only; private briefs and terms are not published. |
| Collectibles and NFTs | Collectible/access/provenance only unless explicit off-chain terms say otherwise. |

## AI And Training Use

Public pages may be indexed by ordinary search engines. Paid, gated,
partner-only, private, and downloadable materials may not be used for model
training, dataset creation, scraping, resale, redistribution, or competing
products without written permission.

The site asserts a text-and-data-mining reservation for protected content under
EU law where applicable.
