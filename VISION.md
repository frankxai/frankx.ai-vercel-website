## Tone & Voice

### ✅ DO Say:
- "My experiments with..."
- "What I'm learning..."
- "Techniques I'm actually using..."
- "My journey mastering..."
- "Raw insights from my creative lab..."
- "This is what's working for me..."
- "I've created 500+ songs..."
- "Follow my transformation..."

### ❌ DON'T Say:
- "12,000+ creators" (unless we actually have that)
- "Enterprise-grade systems" (sounds corporate, not personal)
- "Production-ready frameworks" (too SaaS-y)
- "Cutting-edge generative tools" (marketing speak)
- "Join the movement" (too grandiose)
- Anything that sounds like a company announcement

### Voice Characteristics:
- **Personal**: "I'm Frank" not "We are FrankX"
- **Honest**: "What's working, what's not" not "Revolutionary system"
- **Generous**: "Here's everything I learned" not "Premium content inside"
- **Real**: "500+ songs" not "Thousands of productions"
- **Humble**: "My experiments" not "My frameworks"

---

## Content Philosophy

**AUTHENTICITY OVER POLISH:**
- Share real experiments, including failures
- Document actual workflows I use daily
- No false claims or inflated numbers
- Personal stories over corporate speak

**GENEROUS SHARING:**
- Give away the best techniques for free
- No "teaser" content that leads to paid products
- Open workflows, full prompts, complete systems
- "Here's exactly how I did it"

**TRANSFORMATION FOCUS:**
- My journey learning generative AI
- How I'm evolving as a creator
- Discovering what's possible with these tools
- The creative process, not just the results

## Language Guidelines

### Keywords to USE:
- generative ai, suno music, agentic experiments
- my workflows, my techniques, my discoveries
- creative automation, music production
- learning, transformation, exploration
- personal hub, creative lab, journey

### Keywords to AVOID:
- enterprise-grade, production-ready, cutting-edge
- revolutionary, game-changing, next-generation
- conscious, soul, frequency (spiritual language)
- solutions, platforms, frameworks (corporate language)
- "we", "our team", "our company" (it's just me)

### Claim Integrity:
**ONLY claim what's verifiably true:**
- ✅ "500+ songs with Suno" (I have them)
- ✅ "Oracle AI Architect" (my job title)
- ✅ "Sharing my workflows" (I actually do)
- ❌ "12K community" (don't have exact numbers)
- ❌ "300+ deployed systems" (can't verify)
- ❌ "Thousands of creators" (no data)

## Agent Instructions

When writing content for FrankX.AI:

1. **Write in Frank's voice** - First person, personal, generous
2. **Be specific about what's real** - Only claim what's verifiable
3. **Focus on creative journey** - Not enterprise solutions
4. **Share generously** - Give the best stuff away free
5. **Stay humble** - "My experiments" not "My frameworks"
6. **No false claims** - If you're unsure, ask or skip it
7. **Creative > Corporate** - This is a personal lab, not a company

### Example Good Copy:
"I've been experimenting with Suno for a year now—created over 500 songs and discovered some incredible techniques. Here's exactly what works for me, completely free."

### Example Bad Copy:
"Join 12,000+ creators mastering production-grade AI systems with our enterprise frameworks. Revolutionary tools for the next generation of makers."
