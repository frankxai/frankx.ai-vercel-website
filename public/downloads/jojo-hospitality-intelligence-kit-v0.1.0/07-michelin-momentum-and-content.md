# 07 - Michelin Momentum And Content

Goal: turn attention into clear public communication without hype or overclaiming.

## Public-Safe Baseline

Use sourced facts:

- Harzfenster by Johannes Steingrueber is listed in the 2026 MICHELIN Guide Germany selection as a new one-star restaurant.
- Harzfenster is in Seesen and connected to Hotel Goertler.
- The restaurant presents modern regional cuisine with local quality and broader influences.

Avoid:

- implying FrankX caused the recognition
- exaggerating the award
- publishing private operating details
- using guest names or stories without explicit permission

## Content Pillars

- The house: family, place, Seesen, Harz.
- The craft: menu, product, technique, service.
- The guest path: how to book, what to expect, how to prepare.
- The team: approved human moments.
- The region: local producers and experiences.

## Review Learning

Do not react defensively.

Weekly:

1. Read public reviews.
2. Group patterns.
3. Draft response where useful.
4. Decide what actually needs operational change.
5. Capture one learning note.

## Press And Website Updates

Keep a small approved text block:

```text
Harzfenster by Johannes Steingrueber in Seesen is listed with one MICHELIN star in the 2026 MICHELIN Guide Germany selection. The restaurant is part of the family-run Hotel Goertler and focuses on modern regional cuisine with careful product work and a warm house experience.
```
