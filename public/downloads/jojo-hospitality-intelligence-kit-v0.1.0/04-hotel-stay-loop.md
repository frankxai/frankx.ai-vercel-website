# 04 - Hotel Stay Loop

Goal: make Hotel Goertler, Harzfenster, arrangements, local suggestions, and follow-up feel like one connected house.

## Connected Moments

- booking a room and dinner
- arrival and check-in
- special occasions
- local Harz recommendations
- breakfast and departure
- second dining path
- post-stay follow-up

## Handoff Template

```text
Guest or booking code:
Stay dates:
Restaurant reservation:
Occasion:
Restrictions:
Local interests:
Open questions:
Staff note:
Human decision needed:
```

## Local Recommendation Memory

Keep a small private list:

- short walks
- cultural stops
- weather-safe plans
- family options
- premium local experiences
- producer or craft connections

The goal is not to produce generic travel content. The goal is to make the house's local knowledge easier to use.

## Hotel Follow-Up

Draft only. Human approval required.

```text
Thank the guest.
Mention the stay or dinner only if accurate.
Invite feedback.
Offer a clear path to return.
Keep it warm and brief.
```
