# 08 - Private Data And Guest Trust

Goal: keep trust stronger than convenience.

## Never Put In Public Tools

- guest full profiles
- medical details beyond needed allergy handling
- payment data
- private complaints
- staff HR records
- internal financials
- private supplier terms
- personal family information

## Use Codes Where Possible

For service briefing, use table codes or booking codes where the full name is unnecessary.

## Human Approval Rules

Human approval is required for:

- guest-facing messages
- availability promises
- cancellation or refund decisions
- allergy and safety commitments
- public posts
- review responses
- staff performance notes

## Data Retention

Keep only what the house needs to serve the guest and operate responsibly. Delete stale notes on a defined schedule.

## Vendor Rule

Before adding a new AI or automation tool, answer:

- Where is the data stored?
- Who can access it?
- Can data be deleted?
- Is it needed?
- What is the fallback if the tool fails?
