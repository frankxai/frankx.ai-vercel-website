# 03 - Restaurant Service Loop

Goal: make every service easier to prepare, easier to learn from, and more consistent without making it rigid.

## Daily Service Briefing

Sections:

- tonight's services
- table notes
- restrictions and allergies
- menu changes
- producer or local story focus
- pacing risks
- owner priorities
- guest moments to handle carefully

## Briefing Prompt

```text
Create a concise pre-service briefing for Harzfenster.

Inputs:
- Service date:
- Reservation notes:
- Menu updates:
- Producer notes:
- Staff notes:
- Owner priority:

Rules:
- Keep it short and scannable.
- Flag decisions a human must make.
- Do not invent guest facts.
- Do not include private details that staff do not need.
```

## Post-Service Debrief

Ask:

- What went smoothly?
- What created friction?
- Which table needed more care?
- Which menu explanation landed well?
- What should change tomorrow?
- What should become a training note?

## Weekly Owner Dashboard

Summarize:

- recurring guest questions
- booking friction
- service risks
- review themes
- staff learning needs
- owner decisions due
