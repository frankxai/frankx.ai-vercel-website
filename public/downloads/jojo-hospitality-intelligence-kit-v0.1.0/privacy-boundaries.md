# Privacy Boundaries

This kit is only useful if trust stays stronger than convenience.

## Red Zone

Never place these in public tools or public pages:

- payment data
- passport or identity data
- full medical details
- private family details
- staff HR records
- internal disputes
- private supplier contracts
- guest complaints tied to identity
- unapproved photos or testimonials

## Yellow Zone

Handle carefully and minimize:

- allergies and dietary needs
- repeat-guest preferences
- celebration context
- room-stay details
- staff observations
- internal service failures

Use only when needed for service and keep in approved private systems.

## Green Zone

Generally safer:

- public restaurant facts
- approved menu language
- approved producer stories
- public opening hours
- public awards and listings
- generic workflow templates
- public-safe patterns from reviews

## Approval Gate

Human approval is required before:

- guest-facing communication
- public posts
- review replies
- policy statements
- booking promises
- allergy or safety commitments
- publication of any personal story
