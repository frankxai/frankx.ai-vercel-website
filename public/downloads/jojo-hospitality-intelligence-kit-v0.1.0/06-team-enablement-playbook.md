# 06 - Team Enablement Playbook

Goal: help the team learn faster without adding administrative weight.

## Weekly Team Rhythm

1. Monday or first prep day: owner priorities.
2. Before service: concise briefing.
3. After service: five-minute debrief.
4. Weekly: turn repeated issues into training notes.
5. Monthly: review what to keep, remove, or automate.

## Training Note Format

```text
Situation:
What happened:
Why it matters:
Preferred standard:
Example phrase:
When to ask a manager:
```

## New Team Member Starter Pack

Include:

- house philosophy
- service standards
- common guest questions
- menu explanation examples
- allergy escalation rules
- hotel handoff basics
- what not to say

## AI Boundary For Staff

Staff can use AI to draft, summarize, and prepare. They cannot use AI to:

- invent policy
- promise availability
- diagnose allergy safety
- publish guest stories
- replace manager escalation
