# 02 - Guest And Booking Intelligence

Goal: make demand calmer and guest preparation sharper without outsourcing hospitality.

## What To Track Privately

- Reservation date and time.
- Party size.
- Occasion.
- Allergies and dietary needs.
- Hotel stay connection.
- Special requests.
- Open questions.
- Human owner or manager decision needed.

Do not store more than needed. Do not put payment data or sensitive personal notes into AI tools.

## Booking Reply Draft

Use this structure for human-approved replies:

```text
Thank you for your message.

Confirm what is possible:
[date/time/party/request]

Ask only what is needed:
[allergies, arrival timing, hotel stay, phone number, decision point]

Set expectation:
[response timing, waitlist status, menu format, cancellation note]

Close warmly:
[house tone]
```

## Waitlist Routine

1. Record requested date, party size, contact, and flexibility.
2. Mark priority only with a real reason.
3. Draft a reply that is honest and warm.
4. Update the guest only when there is a real change.
5. Keep waitlist notes private.

## Pre-Arrival Brief

For each service, prepare:

- guest name or table code
- reservation context
- restrictions
- celebration
- timing risk
- hotel connection
- any human decision needed

Keep it short. Staff should be able to scan it in seconds.

## Agent Use

Use `agents/booking-concierge-agent.md` to draft replies and organize details. Never let it send automatically.
