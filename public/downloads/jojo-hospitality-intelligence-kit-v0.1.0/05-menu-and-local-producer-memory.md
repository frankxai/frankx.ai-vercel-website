# 05 - Menu And Local Producer Memory

Goal: make menu language, producer stories, local context, and staff learning easier to maintain.

## What To Capture

- dish name
- key ingredients
- producer or region
- technique
- why it belongs on the menu
- guest explanation in one sentence
- longer story if requested
- allergy or restriction notes
- service risk

## One-Sentence Service Language

Use:

```text
This dish connects [local product or idea] with [technique or contrast], so the guest understands [simple emotional or flavor point].
```

Avoid:

- invented origin stories
- generic luxury words
- language that sounds like a brochure
- claiming sustainability or regionality without proof

## Producer Note Template

```text
Producer:
Product:
Relationship:
Season:
Why it matters:
How staff should explain it:
Public-safe? yes/no
```

## Public Story Boundary

Only publish producer stories, staff details, guest moments, or internal process after approval.
