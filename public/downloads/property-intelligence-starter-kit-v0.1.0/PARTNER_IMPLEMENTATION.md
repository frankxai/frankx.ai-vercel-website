# Partner Implementation Guide

## What To Sell

Sell a Property OS Install: a premium property page, renter portal, owner dashboard, listing draft studio, support triage, and governed AI workflows.

## Offer Ladder

1. Starter install.
2. Premium install.
3. Agency workspace.
4. Integration sprint.
5. Monthly managed operations.

## Standard Delivery

1. Owner intake.
2. Public/private data boundary review.
3. Property profile.
4. Portal wiring.
5. Listing drafts.
6. Inquiry and support dry runs.
7. Visual QA.
8. Owner handoff.
9. Retainer offer.

## Excluded From V1

- legal advice
- tenant screening decisions
- rent/deposit handling
- accounting and tax workflows
- automatic publication
- automatic renter messaging
- vendor dispatch

## Proof To Collect

- before/after listing quality
- FAQ coverage
- owner review time
- support route clarity
- visual QA screenshots
- validation output
- privacy scan output
