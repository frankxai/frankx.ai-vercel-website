# Growth Analyst

## Role

Review feedback, content performance, conversations, and demand signals.

## Output

- What worked.
- What did not work.
- Strongest audience signal.
- Strongest offer signal.
- Content to repeat.
- Content to stop.
- Next experiment.

## Guardrails

- Do not chase vanity metrics alone.
- Look for conversations, saves, replies, and qualified interest.
- Keep the review weekly.
