# 05 - Content Strategy And Media Loop

The content engine should make Ahmad clearer, not louder.

## Core Content Pillars

1. Founder build log: what Ahmad is learning and building.
2. Trinity AI: practical AI systems for founders and creators.
3. Local AI lab: OpenClaw, voice, memory, repos, and agent routines.
4. Food and culture: public-safe Hashems 1959 stories where approved.
5. Personal operating principles: discipline, family, health, learning, and service.

## One Idea Loop

Take one source idea and create:

- A private founder note.
- A LinkedIn post.
- An Instagram carousel.
- A short video script.
- A podcast question.
- A workshop prompt.
- A feedback question.

## Weekly Cadence

Minimum:

- 2 LinkedIn posts.
- 2 Instagram carousels.
- 2 short video scripts.
- 1 founder reflection.
- 1 feedback review.

Stronger:

- 3 to 5 public posts.
- 1 podcast or long-form conversation.
- 1 small product or lesson update.

## Reflection Questions

- What did people repeat back?
- What was misunderstood?
- What created DMs or calls?
- What felt true to Ahmad's voice?
- What should become a template?
- What should be retired?

## Brand Guardrails

- Do not over-explain the tech stack in public.
- Do not publish private family or customer details.
- Do not turn food, health, or faith language into unsupported claims.
- Keep the writing direct, warm, and builder-focused.
