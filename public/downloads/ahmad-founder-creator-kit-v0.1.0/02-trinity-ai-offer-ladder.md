# 02 - Trinity AI Offer Ladder

This ladder gives Ahmad a way to make money while the larger startup becomes clearer.

## Level 1 - Founder Clarity Session

Promise:
Help a founder map their AI opportunity, first workflow, and next two-week build.

Format:
- 60 to 90 minutes.
- Intake before the call.
- One-page operating map after the call.
- Optional follow-up prompt pack.

Why it works:
It is simple, human, and does not require a full platform.

## Level 2 - Local AI Lab Setup

Promise:
Help a founder or creator set up a private AI workstation around voice, code, memory, media, and agent routines.

Tools:
- Mac mini or Mac Studio.
- Codex and Claude.
- OpenClaw or similar local operator layer.
- ACOS packs.
- SIS/SIP memory concepts.
- GitHub repo templates.

Boundary:
Do not promise full autonomy. Start with assisted workflows and human approval.

## Level 3 - Trinity AI Builder Sprint

Promise:
Over 2 to 4 weeks, help a founder choose one AI workflow, build a demo, publish proof, and package a repeatable process.

Deliverables:
- Offer map.
- Repo scaffold.
- Agent briefs.
- Content launch kit.
- Demo review.
- Next roadmap.

## Level 4 - Creator Academy

Promise:
Teach Ahmad's audience how to think, build, and publish with AI tools without losing personal taste or responsibility.

Possible products:
- Short course.
- Workshop.
- Paid community.
- Podcast companion library.
- Download templates.
- Local AI lab walkthrough.

## What To Test First

Run three founder clarity sessions. Track:

- Who asks for the session?
- What language makes them say yes?
- What part of the aftercare note is most useful?
- What should become a template?
- What should not be offered again?
