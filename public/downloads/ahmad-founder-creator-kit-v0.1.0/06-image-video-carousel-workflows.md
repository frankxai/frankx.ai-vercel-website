## Video Script Template

Hook:
Name the concrete problem in one sentence.

Context:
Why this matters to founders or creators.

Build:
Show the practical system, not just opinion.

Proof:
Share an example, demo, or question without overclaiming.

Close:
Invite a reply, call, or next action.

## Carousel Template

Slide 1: Strong title.
Slide 2: The problem.
Slide 3: The old way.
Slide 4: The system.
Slide 5: The tool stack.
Slide 6: The human approval layer.
Slide 7: The first action.
Slide 8: Ask a useful question.

## QA Before Publishing

- Is the claim true?
- Is Ahmad's voice still present?
- Is the call to action clear?
- Is the visual readable on mobile?
- Are private details removed?
- Does the post invite real conversation?

## Loop After Publishing

1. Save comments and DMs.
2. Capture questions.
3. Write a one-paragraph reflection.
4. Update the content library.
5. Decide whether the idea becomes a lesson, offer, or archive.
