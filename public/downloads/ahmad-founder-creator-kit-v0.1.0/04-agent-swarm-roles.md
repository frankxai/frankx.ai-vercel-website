# 04 - Agent Swarm Roles

The swarm is a team of small roles, not a giant autonomous brain.

## Operating Principle

Each agent should do one useful job and leave a clear artifact.

## Roles

- Founder Chief of Staff: turns priorities into a weekly operating plan.
- Repo Accelerator: scaffolds and audits GitHub projects.
- OpenClaw Jarvis Operator: keeps local lab instructions safe and usable.
- Swarm Operator: routes work between agents and prevents conflicting tasks.
- Content Strategist: turns founder ideas into channel plans.
- Image Director: creates image briefs and visual QA criteria.
- Video Producer: turns ideas into scripts, shot lists, and edits.
- Carousel Publisher: builds Instagram and LinkedIn carousel outlines.
- Podcast Producer: plans episode structures and guest arcs.
- Academy Architect: packages lessons and workshops.
- Growth Analyst: reviews performance and feedback.
- Brand Guardian: protects tone, claims, privacy, and taste.

## Routing

1. Founder Chief of Staff receives the raw request.
2. Swarm Operator decides which role should act.
3. One agent drafts.
4. Brand Guardian reviews.
5. Ahmad approves.
6. Growth Analyst captures what happened.

## Human Approval Required

- Public posts.
- Client-facing claims.
- Any use of private story.
- Any medical, legal, financial, or investment language.
- Any automation that changes files, posts online, sends messages, or uses credentials.
