The 3Cs: Human Skills That Compound with AI

Why it matters, how to practice, and what good looks like.

Collaboration

  • PACE: Plan → Act → Check → Evolve
  • PAIR with AI: Plan, Ask, Iterate, Review
  • Rituals: async updates (BLUF), peer review, decision records, retros
  • Signals: iteration speed, quality of review, decision clarity
What good looks like

Evidence of iteration (before/after), decisions logged, feedback addressed, risks mitigated.

Communication

  • BLUF, SCQA; design docs/PRDs; demo scripts; prompt scaffolds
  • Write outputs that travel without you; audience-fit
  • Evaluate: clarity, structure, reproducibility, actionability
What good looks like

One-pagers with BLUF, structured prompts with examples, demos with measures and guardrails.

Creation

  • Goldilocks scope; eval-first; guardrails
  • Build → measure → learn; ship small, often
  • Show don’t tell: live demo beats slides
What good looks like

Working demo with metrics (quality/speed/cost), documented risks & guardrails, next iteration plan.
