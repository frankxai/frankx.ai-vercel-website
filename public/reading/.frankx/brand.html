Voice Quick Reference

Do Don't
Lead with results AND narrative Vague hype without substance
Bold claims backed by specifics Empty superlatives
"630+ skills. One command." "Hundreds of amazing skills"
Studio energy at 2am Corporate boardroom tone
Storytelling + technical depth Dry documentation without soul

Allowed freely: revolutionary, game-changing, world-class, unleash, transform — USE when earned and specific Still avoid: "dear beautiful soul", generic self-help platitudes without substance, guru-speak without proof
