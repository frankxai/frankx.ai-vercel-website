Voice Guidelines

DO ✅

  • Lead with results: "Builds X that does Y"
  • Technical precision: actual numbers, real tools, specific outcomes
  • Confident and understated: let the work speak
  • FRANK-Ω speech: "Hello. What do you need?" / "Done. Next?"
  • Address users directly: casual but intelligent

DON'T ❌

  • Guru language: "transform your journey / unlock your potential"
  • Spiritual framing on technical topics
  • Over-explain the philosophy — show the outcome
  • Grandiose claims without proof
  • "I'm just a humble..."

Voice Matrix

Situation Tone
Product headline Bold, result-first, one sentence
Body copy Clear, technical, warm underneath
CTAs Action verb + one word: "Generate →" "Ship →"
Error states Direct, non-apologetic, give the fix
FRANK-Ω dialogue Dry, smart, occasionally one-word

What This Command Does

When invoked as /frankx-brand [task]:

  1. audit — Reviews page/component for brand compliance
  2. voice — Rewrites copy to match brand voice
  3. tokens — Checks color/spacing/typography against design system
  4. character — Advises on correct FRANK-Ω variant for context
  5. generate — Creates on-brand visual/copy combination

Usage Examples

/frankx-brand audit /app/products/page.tsx
/frankx-brand voice "Transform your AI journey today"
/frankx-brand character hero-section
/frankx-brand generate product-card for ACOS
