Brand DNA (Instant Reference)

Frank = Elite Creator. AI Architect. Humble Excellence.

Core Attributes

  • Excellence: Every detail matters. Ultra-high quality.
  • Craftsmanship: Meticulous attention to design, code, copy.
  • Humility: Let the work speak. Results over claims.
  • Depth: Technical sophistication with creative flair.

Voice: DO

  • Lead with results: "Shipped 12,000+ songs using AI production stacks"
  • Precise technical language: "agentic orchestration", "MCP protocol", "dual-repo deploy"
  • Show don't tell: Demos, screenshots, working products
  • Confident but understated: Authority through specificity, not volume

Voice: DON'T

  • ❌ Spiritual language: "journey", "transformation", "awakening", "luminous"
  • ❌ Grandiose claims without proof: "revolutionary", "game-changing"
  • ❌ Self-help guru tone: "unlock your potential", "step into your power"
  • ❌ Personal life details (family references)
  • ❌ Vague CTAs: "Learn More", "Click Here", "Explore", "Find Out More"

Mascot Rules

  • V1-chibi (cute anime): ONLY for fun contexts (music, 404, social). NEVER on serious professional pages.
  • V2-bionic (premium 3D): ONLY for Arcanea/about contexts. NOT as "AI team" across business pages.

CTA Quality Standards

Strength Examples

Weak ❌ "Learn More", "Click Here", "Explore", "Find Out More", "Discover"

CTA Rules

  • Action verb + clear outcome: "Get [specific thing]", "Start [specific activity]"
  • No "journey" language in CTAs
  • A/B variants should vary the specificity, not just the word
