Design System Overview

Component Hierarchy

  • GlowCard — primary card, cursor-following glow, 12 color variants
  • AuroraGradient — layered radial gradients for hero sections
  • PremiumButton / GlowButton — CTA components
  • SectionDivider — consistent section spacing

Spacing Conventions

  • Section padding: py-24 px-6
  • Wide content: max-w-6xl mx-auto
  • Focused content: max-w-4xl mx-auto
  • Narrow CTA: max-w-3xl mx-auto
  • Card gaps: gap-4 (tight) gap-6 (standard) gap-8 (loose)

Typography Scale

  • Hero: text-6xl md:text-7xl lg:text-8xl font-black leading-[0.9]
  • Section H2: text-4xl font-black
  • Card title: text-base font-bold
  • Body: text-sm text-white/60 leading-relaxed
  • Label: text-xs font-semibold uppercase tracking-[0.25em]
