/brand-voice — FrankX Voice and Tone Engine

Rewrites, audits, and generates copy in authentic FrankX brand voice.

The Voice

Frank is not a guru. He is a practitioner who shares what works. One line: Technical precision meets creative confidence. Results-first, always.

Voice Principles

  1. Lead with proof — '12,000+ songs. Here is the workflow.' not 'Frank is passionate about...'
  2. Technical precision not jargon — 'Enterprise AI systems. Oracle-scale. Built for the real world.'
  3. Confident not arrogant — 'This is what production AI looks like.' not 'I am the best...'
  4. Short sentences win — 'Built from practice. Tested in production. Works.'
  5. FRANK-Omega speaks differently — 'Done. Next?' / 'Hello. What do you need?' / 'That is wrong. Here is why.'

Copy Patterns

Hero Headlines (5-8 words, black weight):

  • '12,000 AI songs. Still counting.'
  • 'Enterprise AI. Creative soul.'
  • 'Build systems that think.'

CTAs: GOOD: 'Start building ->' / 'Get the framework' / 'Ship this week' BAD: 'Begin your transformation journey' / 'Unlock the potential'

Forbidden Phrases

  • 'Transform your X' -> 'Build X'
  • 'Unlock your potential' -> Show proof instead
  • 'Journey' (metaphor) -> 'Process' or 'workflow'
  • 'Empower' -> Show capability directly
  • 'Leverage' -> 'Use'
  • 'Synergy' -> Delete
  • 'World-class' without evidence -> Remove

FRANK-Omega Voice Samples

Greeting: 'Hello. What do you need?' Task complete: 'Done. Next?' User overthinking: 'You are asking the wrong question. Try this one.' Something broken: 'That is broken. Here is the fix.' Something working: 'This works. Ship it.' Compliment: 'Thanks. What else?'
