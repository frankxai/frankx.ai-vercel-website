Brand Experience Overhaul — Axi & Team Integration Plan

Created: 2026-02-20 Status: Planning → Implementation Goal: Transform FrankX.AI from text-heavy site to character-driven premium experience

Executive Summary

The site has 9 premium v2-bionic team characters + 7 Axi mascot variants sitting unused in /gallery and /ai-art. Zero mascot/team presence across 170+ pages. This plan places the right character on the right page to create an immersive, memorable brand experience.

7. Agent Creation Recommendation

Recommendation: Create "Branding Architect" Agent for ACOS

Name: branding-architect (not "Marketing God" — too grandiose for brand voice) Alternative names: brand-engine, growth-architect

Rationale:

  • Existing ACOS agents handle code, content, music — but none handle brand consistency, visual identity, or marketing strategy
  • This agent would ensure every page, component, and content piece maintains brand DNA
  • Not Arcanea-specific, not a "luminor" — a practical builder agent

Agent Profile:

name: branding-architect
role: Brand Experience & Growth Architect
skills:
  [
    frankx-brand,
    web-design-guidelines,
    seo-content-writer,
    marketing-psychology,
    social-content,
  ]
focus: Brand consistency, visual placement, conversion optimization, character integration
triggers: brand, marketing, visual, character, mascot, conversion, CTA
domain_character: Draconia & Draconis (power, growth, conversion)

Key Capabilities:

  1. Audit any page for brand DNA compliance (colors, voice, imagery)
  2. Recommend character placements for new pages
  3. Generate marketing copy in FrankX voice
  4. Optimize CTAs and conversion flows
  5. Maintain character-prompts.json and image generation queue

vs. existing agents:

  • Content Engine (Nova) handles content creation
  • Branding Architect handles brand EXPERIENCE and VISUAL IDENTITY
  • They complement, not overlap
