Capture → Orchestrate → Evaluate (COE) Checklist

Use this to systematize a single workflow. Keep it simple; iterate weekly.

Capture

  • Define inputs, outputs, constraints
  • Write a clear quality bar
  • Collect 10 gold‑standard examples
  • List tools/systems needed (read‑only first)

Orchestrate

  • Chain steps with state (retrieve → generate → transform → format)
  • Sanitize inputs; sign prompts; narrow permissions
  • Add timeouts, rate limits, and budget caps
  • Log inputs/prompts/outputs for traceability

Evaluate

  • Scoring rubric (1–5) mapped to quality bar
  • Sample 10–20 outputs/week
  • Track success, intervention, and incident rates
  • Gate high‑impact actions with human review
