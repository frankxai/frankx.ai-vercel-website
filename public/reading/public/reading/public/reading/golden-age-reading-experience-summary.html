🎨 Design System

  • Headings: Playfair Display (elegant serif, classic)

  • Scale: Responsive font sizes (mobile → desktop)

Colors

  • Primary: Gray-900 / White (high contrast)
  • Accent: Amber-500, Indigo-500 (progress, highlights)
  • Background: White / Slate-950 (light/dark modes)

Style Principles

  • Minimalism + Swiss Style: Clean, spacious, focused
  • High Contrast: 4.5:1 minimum for WCAG AA
  • Generous Whitespace: Breathable layout
  • Smooth Animations: 200-300ms transitions
