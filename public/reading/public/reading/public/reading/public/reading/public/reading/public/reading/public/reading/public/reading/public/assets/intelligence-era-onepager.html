Intelligence Era — One-Pager

This one-pager distills the highest-value frameworks and steps you can use immediately. For the full guide, visit the Resources page on frankx.ai.

Core Idea

Leverage comes from turning repeatable work into systems: Capture → Orchestrate → Evaluate.

COE Framework

  • Capture: inputs, outputs, quality bar, exemplars
  • Orchestrate: chain steps with state and tools
  • Evaluate: rubric (1–5), sample 10–20 outputs/week

Advantage Mapping

  • Skills: domain + workflow design
  • Data: proprietary, structured, consented
  • Distribution: direct channels, trusted surfaces

Risk Compass

  • Safety: content harms, bias, hallucinations
  • Security: injection, exfiltration, secrets
  • Compliance: consent, auditability
  • Societal: fairness, displacement

90-Day Plan

  • Days 1–14: pick workflow, baseline, 10 gold standards
  • Days 15–45: build pipeline, add evaluation, fix failure modes
  • Days 46–75: integrate with real surfaces, add logs/runbooks
  • Days 76–90: limited launch, track success, plan v2
