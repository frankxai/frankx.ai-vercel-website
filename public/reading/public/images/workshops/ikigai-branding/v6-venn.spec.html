V6 Venn Diagram — Premium Editorial Ikigai Venn

CONCEPT

A premium, editorial four-circle Venn diagram for the Ikigai workshop. Four overlapping translucent circles arranged in classic Venn position (top, right, bottom, left). At their intersection, the kanji 生き甲斐 floats — large, elegant, ink-brushed. Around each circle, a small italic serif label names the quadrant. Quiet luxury — Apple keynote restraint meets traditional Japanese ink-on-paper, rendered on dark glass.

ART DIRECTION

Photographic capture of an actual physical diagram printed on a matte dark-glass surface, illuminated by a single warm light source from upper-left. The translucent circles look like overlapping ink washes — ink-violet, ink-amber, ink-emerald, ink-cyan — each at very low opacity. Where they overlap, additive light creates brighter zones. At the center where all four converge, the brightest pooling — almost luminescent — with the kanji 生き甲斐 sitting there in elegant brush calligraphy. Outer labels in restrained serif italic, slightly tilted as if hand-set.

This is NOT a flat digital infographic. NOT a vector illustration. NOT a Canva diagram. Think: a museum-quality ink painting of an Ikigai diagram, photographed on Apple-store-display lighting.

SCENE

Square frame. Four overlapping circles, each ~25% of frame diameter, arranged so each one touches the center point. The four-way overlap forms a small lens-shaped center where 生き甲斐 lives. The kanji is the focal point — composed, anchored, not floating.

Around the outside, just past each circle's outer edge, a single short italic serif label sits — about 8% of frame width from the edge. "love" at top, "good at" at right, "world needs" at bottom, "pays" at left. The labels are quiet, secondary to the visual.

COMPOSITION

1:1 square aspect ratio (Venn diagrams are naturally radial; square framing centers the eye). Diagram occupies roughly 75% of frame, centered. ≥12% breathing room margin on all four sides — generous padding so the diagram never feels cramped. No clutter outside the circles + labels.

LIGHTING

Single soft warm light source from upper-left at ~30° elevation. Casts a faint vignette toward lower-right. NO harsh shadows. NO multiple light sources. NO neon. NO rainbow uplighting. The light feels like a single window in a quiet room at golden hour.

PALETTE

  • Ground: deep matte near-black #0a0a0b
  • Circle fills (each ~12% opacity, additive blend at overlaps):
    • Top circle: ink-violet #6b5a8e
    • Right circle: warm ink-amber #c89865
    • Bottom circle: muted ink-emerald #4a8870
    • Left circle: muted ink-cyan #4a7a8e
  • Center pool (4-way overlap): warm near-white #f4ede0, glowing softly
  • Outer labels: soft white #e8e2d6
  • Kanji at center: warm white with subtle ink texture

Four colors, restrained alphas, additive luminance. NO rainbow. NO saturated primaries.

MOOD

Quiet. Contemplative. Premium. Editorial. The feeling of opening a museum exhibition catalog and finding one perfect diagram printed on heavy paper. Not infographic. Not corporate. Not retreat-poster.

STYLE REFERENCES

  • Apple keynote slide diagrams (clean, restrained, single concept)
  • Linear marketing graphics (matte, premium, dark canvas)
  • Stripe product illustration (geometric clarity, generous space)
  • Traditional Japanese ink-on-paper Venn or mandala-style diagrams
  • NOT Canva. NOT Midjourney "epic". NOT cyberpunk. NOT motivational infographic.

TYPOGRAPHY

The kanji 生き甲斐 in the center — large but not dominating, elegant brush calligraphy or premium Japanese serif (Noto Serif JP equivalent at fontWeight 200). Center kanji is the focal point.

Outer labels — small, italic, editorial serif (Source Serif 4 italic equivalent). Lowercase. Slightly tilted. Subordinate to the circles.

FORBID

Rainbow gradients. Saturated primary colors. Neon. Glow particles. Sparkles. Faux 3D. Drop shadows on circles. Drop shadows on text. Sci-fi UI overlay. "Futuristic HUD." Cyberpunk. Infographic clipart icons inside circles. Stock-photo aesthetic. Vision-board collage. Canva diagram aesthetic. Multiple labels per circle (only one label per quadrant). Text crowding center. Photographic photos of people. Any 3D depth illusion.

FORMAT

1:1 square. 2K minimum (2048×2048). Photographic capture of a real physical ink painting on dark-glass surface. Sharp focus on the diagram. Subtle film grain acceptable but not required.
