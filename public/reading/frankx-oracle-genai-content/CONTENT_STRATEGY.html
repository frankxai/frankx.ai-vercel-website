FrankX Oracle GenAI & AI Agents Content Strategy

Executive Summary

This repository contains FrankX's comprehensive content strategy and assets for Oracle GenAI Services, AI Agents, and production LLM systems on OCI. The content is designed to establish FrankX as an authority in the Oracle AI ecosystem.

Content Pillars

1. Production Systems with Oracle GenAI & AI Agents

  • Audience: Enterprise architects, DevOps engineers, MLOps teams
  • Goal: Establish best practices for production deployments
  • Formats: Architecture Center articles, GitHub samples, playbooks

2. OpenCourse Coding Agents Integration

  • Audience: Developers, engineering teams adopting AI-assisted coding
  • Goal: Connect Oracle GenAI/Grok with opencode, roo-code, kilo-code
  • Formats: Technical guides, integration samples, comparison matrices

3. AI CoE Guidance & Best Practices

  • Audience: AI Centers of Excellence, enterprise AI teams
  • Goal: Provide clear, actionable guidance across Oracle AI offerings
  • Formats: Decision guides, reference architectures, GitHub repositories
