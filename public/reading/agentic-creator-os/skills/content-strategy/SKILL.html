Content Strategy

Purpose

Transform content chaos into strategic clarity. This skill guides the complete content strategy process from audience research through pillar development to distribution planning - ensuring every piece of content serves your larger business objectives.

When to Use This Skill

  • Planning content for a new quarter or year
  • Launching a new brand or product line
  • Rebuilding content strategy from scratch
  • Auditing existing content performance
  • Defining content pillars and themes
  • Creating editorial calendars
  • Mapping content to customer journey

Phase 1: Vision & Objectives

Define Your Content Mission

Answer these questions:

  1. Why does your content exist? (Beyond "marketing")
  2. What transformation do you offer? (Before → After)
  3. What makes your perspective unique? (Unfair advantage)

Phase 2: Audience Research

Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Primary Audience: The Aspiring AI Creator

**Demographics**:
- Age: 28-45
- Role: Creator, entrepreneur, knowledge worker
- Income: $50K-150K
- Location: Global (English-speaking)

**Psychographics**:
- Values: Authenticity, creativity, personal growth
- Fears: Being left behind, losing human touch to AI
- Desires: Creative fulfillment, financial freedom, meaningful work
- Frustrations: Information overload, tech complexity, impostor syndrome

**Behaviors**:
- Platforms: LinkedIn (primary), Twitter, YouTube
- Content preferences: Actionable tutorials, transformation stories
- Purchase triggers: Clear ROI, social proof, money-back guarantee
- Objections: "Is this just another AI grift?"

Audience Segmentation Matrix

Segment Awareness Pain Point Content Need
Curious Unaware Don't know AI can help Educational, inspirational
Exploring Problem-aware Overwhelmed by options Comparisons, guides
Ready Solution-aware Need specific implementation Tutorials, templates
Active Product-aware Optimizing their setup Advanced techniques
