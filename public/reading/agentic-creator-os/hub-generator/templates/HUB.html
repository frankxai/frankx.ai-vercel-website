Purpose

HUB.md is your persistent identity file that Claude reads to understand:

  • Who you are as a creator
  • What makes your voice unique
  • What you're working on
  • What constraints guide your work

Unlike STATE.md (which changes constantly), HUB.md changes rarely - only when your fundamental identity or focus shifts.

Size Guideline

Keep HUB.md under 200 lines. It should answer "Who is this creator?" in one read.

Update Triggers

Update HUB.md when:

  • Your core mission or audience changes
  • You add or complete major projects
  • You make strategic pivots
  • Your content cadence changes
  • You integrate new key platforms

Do NOT update for:

  • Daily work progress (that's STATE.md)
  • Session notes (that's STATE.md)
  • Ideas or todos (that's STATE.md)

Relationship to STATE.md

HUB.md (stable)          STATE.md (fluid)
├── Identity             ├── Current position
├── Mission              ├── Today's focus
├── Voice                ├── Recent decisions
├── Constraints          ├── Pending ideas
└── Projects overview    └── Session continuity

HUB.md = "Who I am" (updates monthly) STATE.md = "Where I am" (updates daily)
