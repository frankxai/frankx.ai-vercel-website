Farcaster/Warpcast Dynamics

Key Differences from Twitter/X:

  • Decentralized: Built on blockchain, censorship-resistant
  • Quality over quantity: Smaller, more engaged audience
  • Technical audience: Higher concentration of builders and developers
  • Channel-based: Content organized into topic-specific channels
  • Onchain actions: Tipping, NFT minting, token interactions
  • Authenticity: Less performative, more genuine discourse
  • Early adopter mindset: Audience values innovation and experimentation

What Works on Farcaster:

  • Technical insights and deep dives
  • Building in public and sharing progress
  • Thoughtful commentary on web3/AI trends
  • Genuine questions and discussions
  • Sharing useful tools and resources
  • Celebrating community wins
  • Transparent thoughts on decentralization

What Doesn't Work:

  • Overly promotional content
  • Engagement farming tactics
  • Shallow takes on complex topics
  • Copy-pasted content from Twitter
  • Hype without substance
  • Ignoring replies and community

Platform-Specific Formatting

Character Limit

  • Standard cast: 320 characters (slightly more than Twitter's 280)
  • Optimal length: 200-280 characters (leaves room for replies)
  • Threads: Use for longer thoughts (each cast = 320 chars)

Visual Elements

  • Emojis: Use sparingly (1-2 per cast) 🟣 ⚡ 🔥 💡 🧵
  • Line breaks: Use for readability (every 1-2 sentences)
  • Purple circle: 🟣 is the unofficial Farcaster emoji
  • Formatting: No bold/italics, use CAPS or "quotes" for emphasis

Links

  • Embeds: Links auto-embed (images, videos, Frames)
  • Frames: Interactive embeds (polls, mints, apps)
  • Placement: Put links at the end of cast

Channels

  • Relevance: Post in relevant channels (/ai, /builders, /crypto)
  • Discovery: Channels help your content reach the right audience
  • Etiquette: Don't spam multiple channels with same cast

Mentions

  • Format: username (no symbol needed in most clients)
  • Engagement: Mention people to start conversations
  • Respect: Don't mention people just for reach

Pro Tips

Timing

  • Best times: 7-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 6-8 PM ET (US-heavy audience)
  • Consistency: Post 2-5x per day (quality over quantity)
  • Threads: Post threads in quick succession (don't space out)

Engagement Hacks

  • Reply fast: Respond to replies within first hour
  • Ask questions: Casts with questions get 3x more replies
  • Use Frames: Interactive Frames get higher engagement
  • Tip quality replies: Use tokens to reward good responses
  • Join channels: Participate in relevant channels actively

Community Building

  • Be genuine: Authenticity is valued over performance
  • Build in public: Share your journey, not just wins
  • Support others: Recast, reply, and celebrate community wins
  • Add value: Share insights, tools, and resources freely
  • Be technical: Don't dumb things down - the audience can handle it

Algorithm (or Lack Thereof)

  • Chronological feeds: No algorithm in most clients
  • Channel-based: Content surfaces in relevant channels
  • Engagement matters: Recasts and replies boost visibility
  • Quality signals: Accounts with engaged followers get more reach
