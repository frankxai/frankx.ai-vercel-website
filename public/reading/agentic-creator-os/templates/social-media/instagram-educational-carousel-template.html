Platform-Specific Formatting

Slide Dimensions

  • Optimal size: 1080 x 1350 pixels (4:5 ratio)
  • File format: JPG or PNG
  • Maximum slides: 10 (use all 10 for max engagement)

Text Readability

  • Font size: Minimum 40pt for body text, 60pt+ for headlines
  • Contrast: High contrast between text and background
  • Font choice: Sans-serif fonts (easier to read on mobile)
  • Text amount: Max 50-75 words per slide

Visual Design

  • Consistency: Use same layout/style across all slides
  • Branding: Include your logo/handle on every slide
  • Colors: Stick to 2-3 brand colors + black/white
  • White space: Don't cram—let content breathe

Caption

  • Length: 1,500-2,200 characters (Instagram's sweet spot)
  • Hashtags: 20-30 relevant hashtags (mix popular + niche)
  • Emojis: Use 3-5 emojis for visual breaks
  • Line breaks: Use blank lines for readability

Pro Tips

Design Tools

  • Canva: Best for beginners (templates + easy editing)
  • Figma: Best for designers (full control)
  • Adobe Express: Good middle ground
  • Template packs: Buy pre-made carousel templates on Etsy/Creative Market

Engagement Hacks

  • Slide 2 tease: "Slide 7 is the game-changer" (drives swipes)
  • Mid-carousel CTA: "Save this now so you don't lose it"
  • Last slide surprise: Add bonus tip on slide 10
  • Caption hook: First line should make them want to read more

Posting Strategy

  • Best times: 9-11 AM, 2-3 PM, 7-9 PM (test for your audience)
  • Frequency: 3-5 carousels per week (mix with other content)
  • Repost: Repost top performers after 3-4 months
  • Stories: Share carousel to stories with "Swipe up to see full post"

Maximizing Reach

  • First hour: Reply to every comment
  • Engagement pods: Share with creator groups (if authentic)
  • Cross-promote: Share on other platforms with teaser
  • Collaborations: Tag relevant accounts (when adding value)

Design Templates by Style

Minimalist Style

  • Clean white or cream background
  • One accent color
  • Simple sans-serif font
  • Lots of white space
  • Subtle icons or graphics

Bold/Vibrant Style

  • Bright, contrasting colors
  • Large, bold typography
  • Gradient backgrounds
  • Eye-catching graphics
  • High energy feel

Professional/Corporate Style

  • Navy, gray, or muted colors
  • Clean, structured layouts
  • Professional fonts (Montserrat, Roboto)
  • Charts or data visualizations
  • Polished, authoritative feel

Playful/Creative Style

  • Fun color combinations
  • Hand-drawn elements
  • Varied fonts (but consistent)
  • Illustrations or doodles
  • Approachable, friendly feel

Customization Checklist

Before posting, verify:

  • All 10 slides created (maximize engagement)
  • Slide 1 hook is attention-grabbing
  • Text is readable on mobile (test on phone)
  • Consistent design across all slides
  • Your handle/logo on every slide
  • Slide 10 has clear CTA
  • Caption expands on carousel content
  • Question asked to drive comments
  • 20-30 relevant hashtags included
  • No typos or design errors
  • Saved as 1080x1350 JPG/PNG
  • Tested swipe flow (does it make sense?)

Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Too much text per slide (overwhelming) ❌ Inconsistent design across slides (looks unprofessional) ❌ Weak hook on slide 1 (people won't swipe) ❌ No CTA on slide 10 (missed opportunity) ❌ Low contrast text (hard to read) ❌ Using all 10 slides for fluff (respect their time) ❌ Generic content (be specific and actionable) ❌ No engagement in caption (kills comments) ❌ Posting without testing on mobile (looks different) ❌ Forgetting to add alt text (accessibility + SEO)

✅ Concise, scannable text (50-75 words max per slide) ✅ Consistent, branded design (professional) ✅ Strong hook that promises value (stops scroll) ✅ Clear CTA that drives action (follow, save, comment) ✅ High contrast, readable text (mobile-first) ✅ Every slide adds value (no filler) ✅ Specific, actionable insights (implementable) ✅ Question in caption (drives comments) ✅ Test on mobile before posting (quality check) ✅ Add alt text to every slide (inclusive + discoverable)

Advanced Techniques

The Cliffhanger Method

  • Slide 5: "But here's where it gets interesting..."
  • Slide 7: "The next slide changed everything for me..."
  • Keeps people swiping to the end

The Callback Pattern

  • Reference slide 1 on slide 10
  • "Remember what I said on slide 1? Here's why it matters..."
  • Creates satisfying loop

The Hidden Gem

  • Slide 8 or 9: "Most people miss this, but..."
  • Rewards people who swipe through
  • Increases completion rate

The Interactive Element

  • Slide 5: "Screenshot this slide"
  • Slide 7: "Save this for later"
  • Slide 9: "Tag someone who needs this"
  • Drives engagement mid-carousel

Turn Your Carousel Into:

  2. LinkedIn Post: Combine into long-form post
  3. Blog Post: Expand each slide into section
  4. YouTube Short: Film yourself teaching each point
  5. TikTok Series: Break into 7 separate videos
  6. Email Newsletter: Send as weekly value
  7. Pinterest Pins: Each slide as separate pin
  8. PDF Lead Magnet: Compile into downloadable guide

Carousel Variations:

  • Update with new examples/data
  • Translate into different language
  • Create "Part 2" with advanced tips
  • Turn into "Mistakes" version (what not to do)

Analytics to Track

Key Metrics:

  • Reach: How many unique accounts saw it
  • Saves: Highest value metric (shows usefulness)
  • Shares: Indicates viral potential
  • Swipe-through rate: % who viewed all 10 slides
  • Comments: Shows engagement quality
  • Profile visits: Indicates interest in you
  • Follows: Direct conversion metric

What to Optimize:

  • If low reach → improve hook (slide 1)
  • If low saves → add more actionable value
  • If low swipes → improve slide 2 context
  • If low comments → ask better questions
  • If low follows → strengthen CTA on slide 10
