Twitter Thread - Expert Positioning Template

Use Case: Establish yourself as an authority on a topic
Time: 15 minutes
Best For: Thought leadership, building following, authority positioning

📋 Template Structure

Tweet 1: The Hook (Setup)

[CONTROVERSIAL CLAIM or SURPRISING STATISTIC about [TOPIC]]

This is [number] years into [your experience/FIELD], and here's what most people get wrong:

🧵 A thread 👇

Tweet 2: The Problem

[Most people/Many experts] believe that [COMMON BELIEF about TOPIC].

But the data tells a different story.

When I [RELEVANT EXPERIENCE], I discovered that [COUNTERINTUITIVE TRUTH].

Tweet 3-5: The Insight (The Meat)

[Number]. [FIRST KEY INSIGHT]

[Explain with concrete example from your experience]

The kicker: [SURPRISING OUTCOME or RESULT]

---

[Number]. [SECOND KEY INSIGHT]

[Story or data that illustrates this]

This matters because [RELEVANCE TO READER]

Tweet 6-7: The Evidence

[Number]. [THIRD KEY INSIGHT - Backed by proof]

[Case study, data point, or research finding]

Key takeaway: [ACTIONABLE LESSON]

Tweet 8: The Synthesis

[Number]. [FINAL INSIGHT - The synthesis]

Everything points to one conclusion:

[YOUR CORE TEACHING/PREDICTION]

Not everyone will agree, and that's the point.

Tweet 9: The Takeaway

So to recap:

• [Key point 1]
• [Key point 2] 
• [Key point 3]

The [TOPIC] landscape is shifting, and those who [ACTION] will [OUTCOME].

Tweet 10: The CTA

If you found this valuable:

1. Follow me @[HANDLE] for more [TOPIC] insights
2. RT the first tweet to share the knowledge
3. [SPECIFIC ACTION - comment, save, etc.]

What's your experience with [TOPIC]? 👇

🎯 Fill-In Guide

Section What to Fill
[CONTROVERSIAL CLAIM] A bold statement that challenges conventional wisdom
[TOPIC] Your area of expertise
[NUMBER] Years of experience or study
[FIELD] Your professional domain
[COMMON BELIEF] What most people think
[COUNTERINTUITIVE TRUTH] What you've learned that contradicts this
[RELEVANT EXPERIENCE] What you did that gave you this insight
[YOUR CORE TEACHING] The main lesson you're sharing
[ACTION] What readers should do
[OUTCOME] What will happen if they take action
[HANDLE] Your Twitter handle

✨ Pro Tips

  1. Personal stories beat statistics - Share your direct experience
  2. Specific beats general - Use real numbers, not round estimates
  3. Contrarian views get shared - Don't be afraid to challenge norms
  4. Questions drive engagement - End with a discussion prompt
  5. Thread locks in followers - People follow for more content like this

📝 Example

Tweet 1: "Most productivity advice is backwards. After 10 years of building startups, I've learned that doing less actually produces more."

Tweet 2: "The optimization culture tells us to squeeze more into every hour. But research on creative work shows the opposite approach works better."

... (continues with 8 more tweets)
