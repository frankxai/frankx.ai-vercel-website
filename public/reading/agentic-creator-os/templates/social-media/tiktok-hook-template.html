TikTok Hook Template

When to Use This Template

  • Creating short-form video content that stops the scroll
  • Capturing attention in the first 1-3 seconds
  • Building viral potential through pattern interrupts
  • Teaching quick tips or insights in under 60 seconds
  • Maximizing watch time and completion rate

  • Trend/Sound (optional): [Trending audio or format to leverage]

The 3-Second Rule

Critical: If you don't hook them in 3 seconds, they scroll.

What Happens in First 3 Seconds:

  1. Visual hook (what they see)
  2. Verbal hook (what you say)
  3. Text hook (on-screen text)

All three must work together to stop the scroll.

Platform-Specific Formatting

Video Specs

  • Aspect ratio: 9:16 (vertical)
  • Resolution: 1080 x 1920 pixels minimum
  • Length: 15-60 seconds (optimal: 21-34 seconds)
  • File format: MP4 or MOV

Text Overlays

  • Font: Bold, sans-serif (easy to read)
  • Size: Large enough to read on mobile
  • Placement: Center or top third (avoid bottom - captions go there)
  • Animation: Use text animations to maintain attention
  • Color: High contrast with background

Captions

  • Auto-captions: Always enable (80% watch without sound)
  • Editing: Fix any auto-caption errors
  • Style: Use TikTok's caption styles for emphasis

Hashtags

  • Count: 3-5 relevant hashtags
  • Mix: 1-2 broad (#contentcreator), 2-3 niche (#AItools)
  • Trending: Include 1 trending hashtag if relevant
  • Placement: In caption, not as on-screen text

Pro Tips

Filming Techniques

  • Lighting: Face a window or use ring light
  • Framing: Center yourself, leave room for text
  • Background: Clean, uncluttered, or relevant to content
  • Movement: Use hand gestures, walk toward camera, show energy

Editing Hacks

  • Jump cuts: Cut out pauses (keeps pace fast)
  • B-roll: Show what you're talking about
  • Transitions: Use quick transitions between points
  • Sound effects: Add subtle SFX for emphasis
  • Music: Use trending sounds when relevant

Engagement Boosters

  • Pin comment: Pin a question or CTA in comments
  • Reply to comments: Respond within first hour
  • Duet/Stitch: Enable for more reach
  • Series: Create "Part 2" for high performers
  • Consistency: Post 1-3x daily for algorithm boost

Algorithm Hacks

  • Watch time: Aim for 100% completion rate
  • Rewatch: Create loop-worthy content
  • Shares: Make it shareable (valuable or entertaining)
  • Saves: Make it save-worthy (reference material)
  • Comments: Ask questions to drive comments

Analyze After 48 Hours:

Advanced Hook Techniques

The Pattern Interrupt

Do something unexpected in first frame:

  • Sudden movement toward camera
  • Unexpected sound or visual
  • Contrarian statement
  • Breaking the fourth wall

The Open Loop

Create curiosity that must be resolved:

  • "Wait until you see what happens next..."
  • "But here's where it gets crazy..."
  • "The last one will blow your mind..."
  • "You won't believe what I discovered..."

The Social Proof Hook

Lead with credibility:

  • "After 10 years in [industry]..."
  • "I've helped 1,000+ people [achieve goal]..."
  • "This got me 1M views..."
  • "Everyone's asking me about this..."

The Urgency Hook

Create FOMO:

  • "Before this gets patched..."
  • "While this is still free..."
  • "Before everyone finds out..."
  • "This won't last long..."

Repurposing Strategy

Turn Your TikTok Into:

  1. Instagram Reel: Same video, adjust caption
  2. YouTube Short: Upload directly
  3. Twitter/X: Upload as native video
  4. LinkedIn: Add professional context
  5. Pinterest Idea Pin: Break into steps
  6. Blog Post: Expand into written content
  7. Email: Embed video in newsletter
  8. Carousel: Turn into Instagram carousel

Variation Strategy:

  • Same content, different hook (test 5 versions)
  • Same hook, different content (series)
  • Longer version for YouTube (2-5 minutes)
  • Behind-the-scenes of making the TikTok

Analytics to Track

Key Metrics:

  • Completion rate: % who watched to end (most important)
  • Watch time: Average seconds watched
  • Shares: Indicates viral potential
  • Saves: Shows value/usefulness
  • Comments: Engagement quality
  • Profile visits: Interest in you
  • Follows: Direct conversion

What to Optimize:

  • Low completion → improve hook or shorten video
  • Low shares → make more shareable (valuable or entertaining)
  • Low saves → add more reference value
  • Low comments → ask better questions
  • Low follows → strengthen CTA
