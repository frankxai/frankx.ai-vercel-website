LinkedIn Thought Leadership Template

Use Case: Establish professional authority and build credibility
Time: 30 minutes
Best For: Positioning as industry expert, professional networking

📋 Template Structure

Opening (Personal Hook)

I [DID SOMETHING UNEXPECTED / LEARNED A SURPRISING LESSON / FACED A CHALLENGE].

And it changed how I think about [PROFESSIONAL TOPIC] forever.

Context (The Situation)

For [TIME PERIOD] working in [INDUSTRY/FIELD], I noticed that [COMMON PATTERN/ PROBLEM everyone accepts].

Most people just [WHAT MOST PEOPLE DO - accept it, work around it, complain about it].

But I couldn't shake the feeling that [YOUR QUESTION/INSIGHT].

The Journey (Your Story)

So I decided to [YOUR ACTION - experiment, study, test, try].

What I found surprised even me:

[DISCOVERY #1 - unexpected result]

[DISCOVERY #2 - counterintuitive finding]

[DISCOVERY #3 - key insight]

The data was clear: [SYNTHESIS OF FINDINGS].

The Lesson (What It Means)

Here's what this means for anyone working in [INDUSTRY]:

[IMPLICATION #1 for their work]

[IMPLICATION #2 for their career]

[IMPLICATION #3 for their approach]

The Proof (Credibility)

I've now [QUANTIFIED RESULT - helped X companies, created Y impact, built Z solutions].

And the pattern holds true across [DIVERSE EXAMPLES to show universality].

This isn't just theory—it's [YOUR CREDENTIALS] speaking.

The Call to Action

If you're [TARGET AUDIENCE - struggling with, exploring, interested in] [TOPIC]:

Here's what I'd suggest:

[Specific actionable advice - 3 bullets]

I'm always happy to [OFFER - connect, discuss, answer questions].

[THOUGHT-PROVOKING QUESTION to spark comments]

Sign-off

What about you?

[Relatable closing thought]

👇 Comment below and let's continue the conversation.

[NAME]
[ROLE] | [COMPANY/INDUSTRY]
[One-line bio or tagline]

🎯 Fill-In Guide

Section What to Fill
[DID SOMETHING UNEXPECTED] A personal story that surprises
[PROFESSIONAL TOPIC] Your area of expertise
[TIME PERIOD] Duration of your experience
[INDUSTRY/FIELD] Your professional domain
[COMMON PATTERN/PROBLEM] What most people accept as normal
[YOUR QUESTION/INSIGHT] What you questioned
[YOUR ACTION] What you did to explore
[DISCOVERY #1-3] Key findings
[SYNTHESIS] What it all means
[QUANTIFIED RESULT] Numbers that prove your point
[DIVERSE EXAMPLES] Different contexts that prove the pattern
[TARGET AUDIONE] Who should care about this
[Specific advice] 3 actionable bullets

✨ Pro Tips

  1. Be specific with numbers - "23% improvement" beats "significant improvement"
  2. Show vulnerability - Admitting struggle builds connection
  3. Use white space - LinkedIn's algorithm rewards readable posts
  4. End with a question - Comments boost reach significantly
  5. Add 3-5 relevant hashtags - But at the very end

📝 Example

Opening: "I once fired our best client. And it was the best decision we ever made."

Context: "For 8 years in agency consulting, I noticed most agencies chase revenue over relationships..."

... (continues with journey, proof, and CTA)
