Product Launch Email Checklist

Pre-Launch

  • Teaser email scheduled 3-7 days before
  • Waitlist/early access list created
  • Launch sequence planned (5 emails)
  • Sales page ready
  • Payment processing tested
  • Customer support prepared

Email Content

  • Clear value proposition
  • Specific benefits (not just features)
  • Social proof included
  • Objections addressed
  • Risk reversal (guarantee)
  • Urgency/scarcity element
  • Multiple CTAs
  • Mobile-optimized

Conversion Elements

  • Launch bonus/incentive
  • Tiered pricing options
  • Clear deadline
  • Money-back guarantee
  • Testimonials/results
  • FAQ section
  • Easy purchase process

Technical

  • All links working
  • Purchase tracking set up
  • Thank you email automated
  • Onboarding sequence ready
  • Refund process documented

Conversion Optimization Tips

Subject Lines

  • Use urgency on final emails
  • Personalize with {{FirstName}}
  • Test emoji vs. no emoji
  • Keep under 50 characters

Email Copy

  • Lead with benefit, not feature
  • Use "you" more than "I"
  • Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
  • Bullet points for scannability
  • Bold key phrases

CTAs

  • Use action-oriented text
  • Make buttons stand out
  • Include multiple CTAs
  • Create urgency

Timing

  • Email 1: 3-7 days before launch
  • Email 2: Launch day, morning
  • Email 3: 2-3 days after launch
  • Email 4: Mid-launch period
  • Email 5: Final day, 6-8 hours before deadline

Performance Benchmarks

Target Metrics

  • Open rate: 25-35%
  • Click rate: 5-10%
  • Conversion rate: 2-5%

Optimization

  • A/B test subject lines
