Community Update Checklist

Content Included

  • Community metrics/growth
  • Member spotlights/wins
  • Recent updates/features
  • Upcoming events
  • Featured content
  • Future plans (transparency)
  • Community needs/opportunities
  • Gratitude section

Engagement Elements

  • Multiple CTAs
  • Questions for feedback
  • Member recognition
  • Discussion prompts
  • Event RSVPs
  • Volunteer opportunities

Tone & Voice

  • Warm and inclusive
  • Transparent and honest
  • Celebratory of wins
  • Inviting participation
  • Personal touch

Technical

  • All links working
  • Event dates correct
  • Member names spelled correctly
  • Images optimized
  • Mobile-friendly

Frequency Recommendations

Monthly Updates

  • Comprehensive overview
  • All sections included
  • Longer format (500-800 words)
  • Scheduled for same day each month

Bi-Weekly Updates

  • Focus on recent highlights
  • Shorter format (300-500 words)
  • More frequent engagement
  • Timely event reminders

Quarterly Updates

  • Big picture overview
  • Strategic direction
  • Major milestones
  • Future vision
  • Longer format (800-1200 words)

Engagement Strategies

Encourage Participation

  • Ask specific questions
  • Create polls or surveys
  • Highlight member contributions
  • Offer volunteer opportunities
  • Recognize active members

Build Connection

  • Share behind-the-scenes
  • Be transparent about challenges
  • Celebrate collective wins
  • Acknowledge individual contributions
  • Create insider moments

Drive Action

  • Clear CTAs for events
  • Easy ways to contribute
  • Opportunities to connect
  • Feedback mechanisms
  • Resource sharing

Performance Metrics

Track These

  • Open rate: Target 25-35%
  • Click rate: Target 5-10%
  • Reply rate: Target 1-3%
  • Event RSVP rate: Target 10-20%
  • Community activity: Increase after email

Optimization

  • Test different update frequencies
  • A/B test subject lines
  • Vary content mix
  • Segment by engagement level
  • Personalize based on activity
