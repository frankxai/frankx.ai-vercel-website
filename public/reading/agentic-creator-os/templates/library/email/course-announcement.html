  • Detailed module breakdown

  • Student testimonials
  • Address "Is this for me?"

  • Your journey and expertise

Course Email Checklist

Content Quality

  • Clear learning outcomes
  • Detailed curriculum
  • Transformation-focused (not just information)
  • Student testimonials
  • Instructor credentials
  • Sample lesson or preview
  • FAQ section

Conversion Elements

  • Early bird pricing
  • Payment plan options
  • Bonuses included
  • Money-back guarantee
  • Urgency/scarcity
  • Multiple CTAs
  • Social proof

Course Details

  • Duration clearly stated
  • Time commitment realistic
  • Format explained
  • Access terms clear
  • Support level defined
  • Prerequisites mentioned
  • Start date confirmed

Technical

  • Enrollment page ready
  • Payment processing tested
  • Welcome email automated
  • Course platform accessible
  • Support system in place

Conversion Optimization

Overcome Common Objections

"I don't have time"

  • Emphasize self-paced nature
  • Break down weekly time commitment
  • Show how to fit it into busy schedule

"Is this worth the investment?"

  • Calculate ROI
  • Compare to alternatives
  • Highlight lifetime access
  • Emphasize guarantee

"Can I really learn this?"

  • Share beginner success stories
  • Explain support available
  • Highlight step-by-step approach
  • Offer preview lesson

"What if I fall behind?"

  • Explain lifetime access
  • Describe community support
  • Share catch-up strategies
  • Emphasize self-paced flexibility
