Instagram Reel Script Template

Reel Length: 15-90 seconds (30-60 optimal)
Format: Vertical (9:16)
Hook Time: First 1-3 seconds

Reel Structure

HOOK (0-3 seconds)

[VISUAL]: [Eye-catching opening]

[TEXT]: [Bold, curiosity-inducing overlay]

[AUDIO]: [Trending sound or voiceover]

Hook Types:

  • Question: "Want to know the secret to [X]?"
  • Bold Statement: "This changed everything..."
  • Pattern Interrupt: [Unexpected visual or action]
  • Relatability: "POV: When you finally [X]..."
  • Transformation: "Before vs. After [X]"

CONTENT (3-55 seconds)

[STRUCTURE OPTIONS]:

Option 1: Tutorial

Hook (3s): "How to [X] in 3 steps"
Step 1 (15s): [Demonstrate with text overlay]
Step 2 (15s): [Demonstrate with text overlay]
Step 3 (15s): [Demonstrate with text overlay]
Result (7s): [Show final outcome]
CTA (5s): "Save this for later!"

Option 2: Transformation

Hook (3s): "Watch this transformation"
Before (10s): [Show starting point]
Process (30s): [Show key steps with transitions]
After (12s): [Reveal final result]
CTA (5s): "Try it yourself"

Option 3: Storytelling

Hook (3s): "This is crazy..."
Setup (15s): [Set the scene]
Conflict (20s): [Build tension]
Resolution (15s): [Payoff]
CTA (7s): "Follow for more stories"

Option 4: Educational

Hook (3s): "3 things you didn't know about [X]"
Point 1 (15s): [Explain with visual]
Point 2 (15s): [Explain with visual]
Point 3 (15s): [Explain with visual]
CTA (7s): "Which surprised you most?"

CTA (55-60 seconds)

[TEXT]: "Follow for more [type of content]"

[VISUAL]: [Engaging gesture or final shot]

[AUDIO]: [Music peak or voiceover]

CTA Options:

  • "Save this for later"
  • "Share with someone who needs this"
  • "Comment [X] if you agree"
  • "Follow for daily [content type]"
  • "Try this and tag me"

Instagram Reel Formats

Format 1: Quick Tip (15-30 seconds)

Best for: Fast value delivery

0-2s: Hook - "Stop doing [X]"
2-12s: Problem - "Here's why..."
12-25s: Solution - "Do this instead..."
25-30s: CTA - "Save this!"

Format 2: Behind-the-Scenes (30-60 seconds)

Best for: Authenticity and connection

0-3s: Hook - "A day in my life as [X]"
3-50s: Show process with multiple clips
50-60s: CTA - "Follow for more BTS"

Format 3: Before/After (20-40 seconds)

Best for: Transformations

0-2s: Hook - "Wait for it..."
2-15s: Before state
15-35s: Transformation process
35-40s: After reveal + CTA

Format 4: Trending Audio (15-30 seconds)

Best for: Viral potential

0-2s: Hook with trending sound
2-25s: Your unique take on trend
25-30s: CTA

Format 5: Carousel Story (60-90 seconds)

Best for: Multiple points or steps

0-3s: Hook - "5 things about [X]"
3-75s: Show each point (15s each)
75-90s: Recap + CTA

Visual Elements

Text Overlays

  • Font: Bold, readable (avoid script fonts)
  • Size: Large enough to read on mobile
  • Color: High contrast with background
  • Position: Center or top third
  • Animation: Subtle, not distracting
  • Duration: Long enough to read comfortably

Transitions

  • Cut: Quick, clean
  • Zoom: Emphasize key moments
  • Swipe: Change scenes
  • Fade: Smooth transitions
  • Effects: Use Instagram's built-in effects

Composition

  • Rule of thirds: Position subject off-center
  • Leading lines: Guide viewer's eye
  • Symmetry: Pleasing to the eye
  • Depth: Foreground/background layers
  • Movement: Keep it dynamic

Audio Strategy

Trending Audio

  • Check Instagram's trending audio daily
  • Use audio with 10K-500K uses
  • Jump on trends early (first 24-48 hours)
  • Add your unique twist

Original Audio

  • Clear voiceover
  • Background music (royalty-free)
  • Sound effects for emphasis
  • Consistent audio branding

Audio Tips

  • Volume balanced
  • No background noise
  • Captions for accessibility
  • Music matches mood

Hashtag Strategy

Mix of 3-5 Hashtags

  • 1 Trending: High volume, current
  • 2-3 Niche: Your specific topic
  • 1 Branded: Your unique hashtag

Hashtag Sizes

  • Large (1M+ posts): Trending, competitive
  • Medium (100K-1M): Niche, targeted
  • Small (10K-100K): Specific, engaged

Placement

  • End of caption (not first line)
  • Or first comment
  • Don't overdo it (3-5 max)

Engagement Optimization

Troubleshooting

Low Reach

  • Improve hook (first 3 seconds)
  • Use trending audio
  • Post at peak times
  • Engage immediately after posting
  • Use relevant hashtags

Low Engagement

  • Add clear CTA
  • Ask questions in caption
  • Make content more relatable
  • Reply to all comments
  • Create shareable content

Low Saves

  • Increase educational value
  • Make content reference-worthy
  • Add "Save this" CTA
  • Create tutorial content
  • Provide actionable tips
