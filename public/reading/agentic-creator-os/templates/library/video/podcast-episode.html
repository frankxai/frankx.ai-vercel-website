INTERVIEW FORMAT

SEGMENT 1: Background (3:00-15:00) - 12 minutes

HOST: [Guest], thanks for joining me. Let's start with your story. How did you get into [field/topic]?

GUEST: [Response]

HOST: [Follow-up question based on response]

[QUESTIONS TO COVER]:

  • Origin story
  • Key turning points
  • Early challenges
  • Formative experiences

[TRANSITION]: That's fascinating. So that led you to [next topic]...

SEGMENT 2: Deep Dive (15:00-40:00) - 25 minutes

HOST: Let's talk about [main topic]. Walk me through [specific aspect].

[QUESTIONS TO EXPLORE]:

  • Core methodology/approach
  • Specific examples
  • Lessons learned
  • Mistakes made
  • Unexpected insights
  • Contrarian views

[FOLLOW-UP PROMPTS]:

  • "Tell me more about that..."
  • "What do you mean by [X]?"

  • "What would you do differently?"

SEGMENT 3: Practical Application (40:00-55:00) - 15 minutes

HOST: This is all great, but let's make it actionable. If someone's listening and wants to [achieve outcome], where should they start?

[QUESTIONS]:

  • First steps
  • Common mistakes to avoid
  • Resources needed
  • Timeline expectations
  • Success metrics

HOST: What about [specific challenge]? How do you handle that?

GUEST: [Response]

SEGMENT 4: Rapid Fire (55:00-65:00) - 10 minutes

HOST: Alright, let's do some rapid-fire questions. Just say the first thing that comes to mind.

QUESTIONS:

  1. Best book you've read recently?
  2. One tool you can't live without?
  3. Biggest mistake you've made?
  4. Best advice you've received?
  5. What's overrated in your industry?
  6. What's underrated?
  7. One thing you wish you knew 10 years ago?
  8. What are you working on next?

Question Bank

Opening Questions

  • "Tell me your story. How did you get here?"
  • "What's the most important thing you've learned about [topic]?"
  • "What's the biggest misconception about [topic]?"
  • "Walk me through a typical day in your life."

Deep Dive Questions

  • "Can you break that down for me?"
  • "What does that actually look like in practice?"
  • "Tell me about a time when [X]."
  • "What surprised you most about [topic]?"
  • "What do most people get wrong about this?"

Challenging Questions

  • "What's your most controversial opinion about [topic]?"
  • "What's something you believed that you no longer believe?"
  • "What's your biggest failure and what did you learn?"
  • "If you could start over, what would you do differently?"

Future-Focused Questions

  • "Where do you see [industry/topic] going?"
  • "What are you most excited about?"
  • "What keeps you up at night?"
  • "What's next for you?"

Closing Questions

  • "What advice would you give your younger self?"
  • "What's one thing listeners should do today?"
  • "What question should I have asked that I didn't?"
  • "Where can people learn more from you?"

Production Checklist

Recording

  • Quiet environment
  • Good microphone quality
  • Backup recording running
  • Headphones on
  • Water nearby
  • Notes accessible
  • Recording levels checked

Post-Production

  • Audio cleaned (noise reduction)
  • Levels normalized
  • Intro/outro added
  • Ads inserted (if applicable)
  • Chapters/timestamps added
  • ID3 tags complete
  • Cover art attached

Publishing

  • Episode uploaded to host
  • Show notes written
  • Timestamps added
  • Links verified
  • SEO optimized
  • Social media graphics created
  • Audiogram clips created
  • Email to list sent
  • Social posts scheduled
