TikTok Tutorial Script Template

Video Length: 15-60 seconds
Hook Time: First 1-2 seconds
Format: Vertical (9:16)

TikTok Script Structure

HOOK (0-2 seconds)

[VISUAL]: [Attention-grabbing opening shot]

[TEXT OVERLAY]: [Bold, curiosity-inducing text]

[YOU]: [Compelling opening line]

Examples:

  • "This changed everything..."
  • "Stop doing [X]. Do this instead..."
  • "I wish I knew this sooner..."
  • "The [X] hack nobody talks about..."
  • "POV: You just discovered [X]..."

PROMISE (2-5 seconds)

[YOU]: "I'm going to show you how to [specific outcome] in [timeframe]"

[TEXT]: [Reinforce the promise]

Examples:

  • "How to [X] in 30 seconds"
  • "The easiest way to [X]"
  • "3 steps to [X]"

CONTENT (5-50 seconds)

Step 1 (5-15 seconds)

[TEXT]: "Step 1: [Action]"

[VISUAL]: [Demonstrate the step]

[YOU]: [Brief explanation]

[TRANSITION]: [Quick cut or effect]

Step 2 (15-30 seconds)

[TEXT]: "Step 2: [Action]"

[VISUAL]: [Demonstrate]

[YOU]: [Brief explanation]

[TRANSITION]: [Quick cut or effect]

Step 3 (30-45 seconds)

[TEXT]: "Step 3: [Action]"

[VISUAL]: [Demonstrate]

[YOU]: [Brief explanation]

RESULT (45-55 seconds)

[VISUAL]: [Show the final result]

[TEXT]: "And that's it!"

[YOU]: [Quick recap or benefit statement]

CTA (55-60 seconds)

[TEXT]: "Follow for more [type of content]"

[YOU]: "Save this for later!"

[VISUAL]: [Point to follow button or engaging gesture]

TikTok Format Variations

Format 1: Quick Tip

Length: 15-30 seconds

Hook (2s): "Stop wasting time on [X]"
Tip (10s): "Do this instead..."
Result (5s): "You're welcome"
CTA (3s): "Follow for more"

Format 2: Step-by-Step

Length: 30-60 seconds

Hook (2s): "How to [X] in 3 steps"
Step 1 (15s): [Demonstrate]
Step 2 (15s): [Demonstrate]
Step 3 (15s): [Demonstrate]
Result (8s): [Show outcome]
CTA (5s): "Save this!"

Format 3: Before/After

Length: 20-40 seconds

Hook (2s): "Watch this transformation"
Before (8s): [Show problem]
Process (15s): [Show solution]
After (10s): [Show result]
CTA (5s): "Try it yourself"

Format 4: Myth Busting

Length: 30-45 seconds

Hook (2s): "Everyone gets this wrong"
Myth (10s): "People think [X]"
Truth (15s): "But actually [Y]"
Proof (10s): [Demonstrate]
CTA (5s): "Follow for more truths"

Format 5: Trending Sound

Length: 15-30 seconds

Hook (2s): [Trending sound starts]
Content (20s): [Your take on trend]
Twist (5s): [Your unique angle]
CTA (3s): [Engagement prompt]

Text Overlay Strategy

First Frame: Bold, attention-grabbing text
Throughout: Key points as text
Last Frame: CTA text

Best Practices:

  • Large, readable font
  • High contrast colors
  • Minimal words per frame
  • Emojis for emphasis
  • Consistent style

Sound Selection

Trending Sounds:

  • Check TikTok's trending page daily
  • Use sounds with 10K-100K uses (sweet spot)
  • Jump on trends early

Original Sounds:

  • Clear audio quality
  • Engaging voice
  • Background music (if applicable)
  • Consistent volume

Viral Elements Checklist

  • Hook in first 1-2 seconds
  • Fast-paced editing
  • Text overlays for context
  • Trending sound or original audio
  • Clear value proposition
  • Relatable content
  • Pattern interrupt every 3-5 seconds
  • Strong CTA at end
  • Optimized caption
  • Strategic hashtags

Engagement Tactics

In-Video

  • Ask questions
  • Create suspense
  • Use humor
  • Show personality
  • Be authentic
  • Add unexpected twists

In Caption

  • Ask for opinions
  • Request tags
  • Encourage duets/stitches
  • Prompt saves
  • Ask questions

In Comments

  • Reply to every comment (first hour)
  • Ask follow-up questions
  • Create conversation
  • Pin best comment
  • Engage with other creators

Content Ideas

Tutorial Types

  1. Quick Hacks: "The [X] hack that changed my life"
  2. Common Mistakes: "Stop doing [X], do this instead"
  3. Step-by-Step: "How to [X] in 3 easy steps"

  5. Comparisons: "[X] vs [Y]: Which is better?"
  6. Behind-the-Scenes: "How I actually [X]"
  7. Myth Busting: "The truth about [X]"

  9. Beginner Guides: "[X] for complete beginners"
  10. Pro Tips: "Advanced [X] tips nobody shares"

Posting Strategy

Best Times to Post

  • 6-10 AM: Morning scroll
  • 12-2 PM: Lunch break
  • 7-11 PM: Evening peak

Test your audience: Analytics will show your best times

Posting Frequency

  • Minimum: 1 per day
  • Optimal: 2-3 per day
  • Maximum: 5 per day

Consistency > Quantity

Content Mix

  • 40% Educational
  • 30% Entertaining
  • 20% Trending
  • 10% Promotional

Production Tips

Equipment

  • Camera: Smartphone (vertical mode)
  • Lighting: Natural light or ring light
  • Audio: Built-in mic or lapel mic
  • Tripod: For stability

Filming

  • Vertical format: 9:16 ratio
  • Resolution: 1080x1920 minimum
  • Frame rate: 30fps or 60fps
  • Lighting: Front-facing, even
  • Background: Clean, uncluttered

Editing

  • App: TikTok native editor or CapCut
  • Cuts: Every 2-3 seconds
  • Effects: Subtle, purposeful
  • Transitions: Quick, smooth
  • Text: Large, readable
  • Music: Trending or fitting

Analytics to Track

Key Metrics

  • Views: Total reach
  • Watch Time: Average % watched
  • Engagement Rate: Likes + comments + shares / views
  • Follower Growth: New followers per video
  • Traffic Source: FYP vs Following

Success Indicators

  • Watch Time: 70%+ is excellent
  • Engagement: 5%+ is good
  • Shares: High viral potential
  • Saves: High value content
  • Profile Visits: Strong hook

Troubleshooting

Low Views

  • Improve hook (first 2 seconds)
  • Use trending sounds
  • Post at peak times
  • Engage with comments immediately
  • Check video quality

Low Engagement

  • Add clear CTA
  • Ask questions in caption
  • Make content more relatable
  • Respond to all comments
  • Create conversation starters

Not on FYP

  • Use relevant hashtags
  • Complete your profile
  • Post consistently
  • Engage with others
  • Create original content
  • Follow trends
