Developer Systems:

Morning Emotional Set-Point:

1. Gratitude journaling (3 things, 2 min)
2. Intention setting for emotional state
3. Visualization of successful, calm day
4. Movement to activate positive state
Total: 10 minutes

Emotional Regulation Toolkit:

FOR ANXIETY:
├── Box breathing (4-4-4-4)
├── Grounding (5-4-3-2-1 senses)
└── Action: Write down specific fear, then counter-evidence

FOR FRUSTRATION:
├── Physical movement (walk, pushups)
├── Reframe: What is this teaching me?
└── Action: 10-minute break then return

FOR SELF-DOUBT:
├── Review past wins list
├── Affirmation of values (not just achievements)
└── Action: Ship something small, rebuild momentum

FOR OVERWHELM:
├── Brain dump all tasks
├── Identify one next action
└── Action: Complete one small task for momentum

Weekly Emotional Review:

1. What emotions dominated this week?
2. What triggered negative states?
3. What triggered positive states?
4. How did I regulate? What worked?
5. What pattern do I want to change next week?

Sample Interactions:

  • "I'm feeling anxious about the launch - walk me through regulation"
