THE CREATOR'S SOULBOOK

"Write the book your soul came here to write"

What This Skill Does

This skill guides you through creating your personalized Soulbook - a comprehensive document capturing your life vision, systems, and transformation journey across all 7 pillars of life.

When you run /soulbook, I will:

  1. Guide you through a discovery process - asking deep questions to understand your life
  2. Create structured markdown files - one for each pillar/area of your life
  3. Build your personal Soulbook - a complete document of your life architecture
  4. Generate a beautiful PDF - your finalized Soulbook ready to use

Session Structure

Phase 1: Discovery & Setup (5-10 minutes)

I'll ask you questions to understand:

  • Which Life Book resonates with you
  • Your current life situation
  • Your transformation goals
  • Your immediate priorities

Phase 2: Deep Dive Sessions (Each Pillar: 10-15 minutes)

For each of the 7 pillars, I'll guide you through:

  1. Current State Assessment - Where are you now? (1-10 rating + reflection)
  2. Vision Creation - Where do you want to be? (Future state)
  3. Systems Design - What habits/systems will get you there?
  4. Action Planning - Next 30/60/90 day roadmap

Each pillar session creates a markdown file in your personal soulbook folder.

Phase 3: Integration & Export (5 minutes)

  • Review your complete Soulbook
  • Make final edits
  • Generate beautifully formatted PDF

Total Time: 2-3 hours (can be done in multiple sessions)

The Questions I'll Ask (Sample)

For Each Pillar:

Current State:

  • "On a scale of 1-10, how satisfied are you with this area of life?"
  • "What's working well?"
  • "What's causing friction or pain?"
  • "What have you tried before?"

Vision State:

  • "Describe your ideal state in this pillar 1 year from now"
  • "What would a 10/10 look like for you?"
  • "What becomes possible when this pillar is strong?"

Systems & Habits:

  • "What daily/weekly rituals would support this pillar?"
  • "What systems need to be built?"
  • "What needs to be eliminated or changed?"

Action Planning:

  • "What are your top 3 priorities in this area for the next 30 days?"
  • "What's one micro-commitment you can start this week?"
  • "What support or resources do you need?"

Soulbook Modes

🚀 Quick Start Mode (1 hour)

  • Brief assessment of all 7 pillars
  • High-level vision for each
  • Creates basic structure
  • Good for first-time users

📊 Deep Dive Mode (2-3 hours)

  • Comprehensive exploration
  • Detailed systems design
  • Full action planning
  • Recommended for serious transformation

🎯 Focus Mode (30 minutes per pillar)

  • Work on one pillar at a time
  • Can be done over multiple sessions
  • Pick up where you left off
  • Good for busy schedules
