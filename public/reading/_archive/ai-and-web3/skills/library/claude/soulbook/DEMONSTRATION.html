## Systems & Habits

### Daily Rituals
1. **Morning Stack (5:30-6:30am)**
   - Wake naturally (no alarm)
   - 16oz water + electrolytes
   - 10min mobility routine
   - Nutrient-dense breakfast (40g protein)

2. **Energy Management**
   - No caffeine after 12pm
   - Power snack at 2pm (prevents crash)
   - Evening walk after dinner (15min)

3. **Sleep Protocol**
   - Blue blockers after 8pm
   - Phone away by 9pm
   - Read for 30min before bed
   - Lights out by 10pm

### Weekly Practices
- Mon/Wed/Fri: Strength training (6am, 60min)
- Tue/Thu: Cardio (HIIT or zone 2, 30min)
- Sat: Active recovery (hike, yoga, swim)
- Sun: Complete rest
- Weekly meal prep Sunday 2pm

### What to Build
1. Meal prep system (batch cooking Sunday)
2. Home gym cardio setup (rower or bike)
3. Recovery toolkit (massage gun, bands, foam roller)
4. Energy tracking system (daily 1-10 rating)

### What to Eliminate
1. Weekend alcohol binges → max 2 drinks Sat
2. Late-night snacking → kitchen closes at 8pm
3. Phone in bedroom → charging station in kitchen
4. Energy drinks → only black coffee before noon
