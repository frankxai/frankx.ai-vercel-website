Panel Notes

Skeptical builders preferred Variant A because it sounded least like a funnel. They asked for a more explicit method.

Busy executives and technical operators preferred Variant B because the three-filter method is easy to forward and reuse.

High-intent buyers responded to Variant C, but the offer bridge arrived too early for a trust-building weekly issue.

Paid member and enterprise panels wanted clearer approval language, evidence capture, and less automation-for-automation language.

Decision

Use Variant A as the emotional spine, insert Variant B's three-filter method, and keep Variant C's offer bridge as a short P.S.

The final issue should optimize for replies, saves, and trust. Beehiiv and Resend live sends remain blocked until human approval.
