FrankX Newsletter Intelligence System V1

The repo is the source of truth for issue drafts, variants, simulation reports, approval packets, and archive rendering.

Resend remains the transactional layer for welcome email, confirmation, unsubscribe, contact updates, topic tags, and test sends.

Beehiiv is the preferred growth and publishing layer once the cockpit approval loop is reliable.

Substack is not the V1 backend. It can be revisited as a mirror only if the audience strategy needs it.

Operating Loop

  1. Choose the weekly pillar.
  2. Draft three variants.
  3. Simulate against reader panels.
  4. Pick a winner or hybrid.
  5. Build an approval packet.
  6. Publish only after a human gate.
  7. Feed replies and analytics into the next plan.

Weekly Pillars

  • Strategy: what to build and why it compounds.
  • Talent: who does the work, including human and agent roles.
  • Technology: the stack, interfaces, workflows, and failure modes.
  • Data: memory, evidence, instrumentation, and feedback loops.
  • Ethics: governance, consent, claims, and trust.
  • Case studies: proof through concrete working systems.

Brand Streams

  • FrankX Newsletter: central weekly operating note.
  • Builder Lab Primer: educational sequence for working artifacts.
  • AI Architect Dispatch: operator and system architecture signal.
  • Music Lab: creative production and taste experiments.
  • GenCreator Stack: creator operating system and tooling.
  • Inner Circle Brief: private member loop and deeper decisions.
  • Investor Intelligence: strategy, markets, and capital allocation.
  • Arcanea Transmissions: mythic creative lab, kept separate from core business claims.
