# FrankX /mind Page Spec

## Outcome Contract

- Surface: `https://frankx.ai/mind`
- Audience: AI builders, AI Architects, technical creators, and founders using agents in serious work.
- First read: The human operator is the first operating system behind every AI workflow.
- Brand mode: FrankX command layer. Direct, technical, practical, emotionally honest without guru language.
- Primary asset tier: Tier B generated-owned cinematic image system plus a lightweight Three.js/R3F metaphor.
- Core metaphor: Attention, memory, taste, and feedback loops turn noisy inputs into shipped systems.

## Creative Thesis

The page should feel like entering a quiet cognitive workshop at night: an obsidian workbench where scattered signals become models, models become systems, and systems become public work.

## Direction Board

Keep:

- Obsidian and graphite material language.
- Thin emerald and cyan signal lines.
- Tactile objects: lenses, plates, notes, architecture models, instruments, work surfaces.
- Human presence through evidence of work, not portraits.
- Calm spacing and stable typography.

Avoid:

- Brains, heads, faces, robots, cute assistants, generic neural blobs.
- Spiritual, guru, clinical, or self-help language.
- In-image text, fake UI, fake charts, fake logos, warped hands.
- Purple-blue SaaS fog, decorative orbs, particle spam.
- Motion on headings, paragraphs, or CTAs.

Signature:

- "Mind Lattice": a quiet 3D operating diagram where inputs route through attention, memory, taste, and review into shipped artifacts.

## Page Structure

1. Full-bleed hero: generated workbench image, stable text, primary CTA, lightweight 3D lattice overlay.
2. Operator stack: five modules of the human OS.
3. Scroll story: noise to model to system to shipped artifact.
4. Ten-frame image system: every generated image has a page role.
5. Protocols: practical working rules for attention, memory, taste, and review.
6. What this is not: not therapy, guru advice, productivity theater, or a clinical claim.
7. CTA: newsletter and Architecture of Intelligence path.

## Motion Brief

- Motion job: show signal routing and system state, not decoration.
- Runtime: R3F/Three for the lattice, Motion for reduced-motion detection only.
- Desktop behavior: slow lattice rotation and short signal pulses.
- Mobile behavior: static composition first; canvas remains simple and unframed.
- Reduced motion: disable canvas animation and show static diagram/poster.
- Performance budget: no GLB, no postprocessing, no unbounded particles, DPR capped at 1.5.
