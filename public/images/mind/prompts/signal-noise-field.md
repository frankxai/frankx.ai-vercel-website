## CONCEPT
Signal and noise: the operator creates a clean path through a turbulent input field.

## ART DIRECTION
Cinematic FrankX signal field. Dark spatial depth, graphite fragments, emerald/cyan route geometry, not a generic neural network.

## SCENE
A wide dark field of scattered matte fragments and faint data-like particles. A single clean luminous route cuts through the field and organizes nearby fragments into a coherent path. The route ends at a small stable system object.

## COMPOSITION
16:9 crop. Wide panoramic scene with strong diagonal movement. The path should be readable immediately, with calm negative space around it.

## LIGHTING
Low volumetric light, cyan glints on fragments, emerald route line, subtle fog only for depth, no overglow.

## PALETTE
Obsidian, charcoal, smoke, emerald #10b981, cyan #06b6d4, restrained silver.

## MOOD
Clarity under pressure. Calm control inside complexity.

## CONSTRAINTS
No readable text, no logos, no human silhouette, no brain, no robot, no globe, no stock data tunnel, no purple-blue gradient fog, no random particles as primary subject. Ready for immediate critic.
