## CONCEPT
AI as a mirror for the operator: not a replacement self, a precise reflective instrument.

## ART DIRECTION
FrankX black-glass instrument design. Reflective but grounded, technical, personal without portraits, emotionally quiet.

## SCENE
A vertical smoked-glass diagnostic surface stands on an obsidian desk. In the reflection: abstract work artifacts, signal routes, memory plates, and a clean system topology. The physical foreground includes a small input device, paper notes with no readable text, and a thin cyan edge light.

## COMPOSITION
2:3 vertical crop. Mirror panel dominates the upper center. Foreground table creates tactile grounding. Leave dark margins for mobile crop.

## LIGHTING
Soft black-room lighting, cyan edge on the mirror, emerald micro-lines in the reflected route, realistic reflections without showing any face or body.

## PALETTE
Obsidian, smoked glass, graphite, emerald #10b981, cyan #06b6d4, pale silver.

## MOOD
Honest, reflective, focused. It should feel like a tool that helps you see your work more clearly.

## CONSTRAINTS
No face, no body, no hands, no portrait, no readable text, no labels, no logos, no brain, no neurons, no robot, no humanoid reflection, no spiritual mirror, no ghostly figure. Ready for immediate critic.
