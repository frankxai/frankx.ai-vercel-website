## CONCEPT
Taste as a calibration table: references, rejected options, and a refined decision.

## ART DIRECTION
FrankX editorial studio. High-restraint composition with tactile print samples, black glass panels, thin metal tools, and subtle signal color.

## SCENE
A dark table with three zones: rough options on the left, a precise comparison area in the middle, and one refined artifact on the right. Objects are abstract and visual, with no readable text. Include small graphite rulers, clean paper swatches, glass overlays, and one luminous decision path.

## COMPOSITION
4:3 crop. Overhead but slightly angled. The refined artifact should be the clear focal point. Use quiet space, not a busy moodboard.

## LIGHTING
Soft studio light, thin cyan edge reflections, tiny emerald highlight on the selected route, deep shadow around unused options.

## PALETTE
Black, graphite, off-white paper, smoked glass, emerald #10b981, cyan #06b6d4.

## MOOD
Editorial, considered, disciplined, humane.

## CONSTRAINTS
No readable text, no logos, no faces, no hands, no brain, no robot, no messy collage, no Pinterest board look, no colorful sticky notes. Ready for immediate critic.
