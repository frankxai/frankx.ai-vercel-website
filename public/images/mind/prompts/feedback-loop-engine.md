## CONCEPT
The feedback loop engine: observe, build, test, refine, and ship.

## ART DIRECTION
FrankX mechanical systems language. Precision engine meets software workflow, black metal, smoked glass, controlled signal lines.

## SCENE
A compact circular mechanical engine on a black workbench, made of four connected stations. Each station is represented by abstract tools and panels, no labels. A small route line circulates through the engine and exits as a clean artifact.

## COMPOSITION
16:9 crop. Engine sits center-right. Exit route moves toward lower right. Negative space on left for page layout compatibility.

## LIGHTING
Focused overhead light, graphite shadows, emerald pulse along the loop, cyan highlights on mechanical edges.

## PALETTE
Obsidian, brushed graphite, smoked glass, emerald #10b981, cyan #06b6d4, tiny off-white highlights.

## MOOD
Operational, repeatable, grounded. The image should feel like process discipline made visible.

## CONSTRAINTS
No readable text, no logos, no people, no hands, no brain, no robot, no factory stock image, no steampunk, no neon overload. Ready for immediate critic.
