## CONCEPT
Premium cinematic hero frame for FrankX /mind: the human operator as the first operating system behind AI work. The scene is an obsidian cognitive workshop where noisy inputs become clean architecture.

## ART DIRECTION
FrankX command-layer aesthetic. Dark graphite and black glass, precise emerald #10b981 and cyan #06b6d4 signal lines, brushed metal, smoked glass, paper notes, and quiet technical luxury. It should feel like Vercel restraint plus Apple Pro cinema, not a generic AI poster.

## SCENE
A high three-quarter view of a large black workbench in a night studio. On the table: a precision attention lens, layered graphite memory plates, a black-glass model architecture, a visible decision bus, a few paper notes with no readable text, and thin signal traces routing toward a clean output object. Emerald is the active path; cyan is reference and context. No people, no face, no brain, no robot.

## COMPOSITION
16:9 hero background. Strong negative space on the left third for page copy. Main object cluster on the right and lower center. The next-section hint should feel possible below the fold. Cinematic depth, inspectable subject, no clutter.

## LIGHTING
Low-key studio lighting with a soft overhead rim, small cyan highlights on glass edges, faint emerald signal glows, warm practical lamp reflection on metal, subtle film grain.

## PALETTE
Obsidian #0a0a0b, graphite #111113, smoked glass, off-white highlights, emerald #10b981, cyan #06b6d4, tiny warm amber only as desk reflection.

## MOOD
Focused, precise, intimate, late-night studio energy. Emotion comes from care and craft, not sentimentality.

## CONSTRAINTS
No readable text, no labels, no logos, no typography, no people, no face, no hands, no brain, no head, no neurons, no robot, no circular portrait, no generic neural network, no floating orb, no purple-blue SaaS fog, no stock laptop scene, no impossible reflections. Ready for immediate critic.
