## Variant 1 — primary

Five abstract glyph-symbols arranged in a horizontal flow, connected by amber arrows that progressively brighten left-to-right. Void `#0a0a0b` foundation. Each glyph is contained in its own subtle circular frame, ~140px equivalent, evenly spaced.

The five symbols, in order:

1. **Father (Vater)** — a single solid circle, dense, the source. Pale `#E8DDD0`.
2. **Inheritance (Erbe)** — a circle dissolving into multiple smaller circles, the gift breaking apart for handing.
3. **Synthesis (Synthese)** — interlocking ring shapes, the small circles being woven into a new pattern.
4. **System (System)** — a hexagonal lattice, the woven pattern stabilized into structure.
5. **Transmission (Weitergabe)** — the lattice opening up at the right edge, releasing into a glow that exits the frame.

Connecting arrows between the five glyphs: thin amber lines with small chevron arrowheads. The arrows progressively gain weight and saturation from Father (faint) to Transmission (most luminous), suggesting accumulated transformation.

Below each glyph, leave deliberate empty space for the React-rendered label (German + English).

Composition: horizontal flow, left-to-right reading, generous breathing room above and below the chain. The leftmost glyph has slight shadow weight; the rightmost glyph has slight outward glow — visual asymmetry of "received" vs "given onward".

Color palette: void `#0a0a0b`, soul-amber `#f59e0b` for the arrows and right-side glow, pale `#E8DDD0` for the leftmost (Father) glyph, soul-amber-dark `#d97706` for the lattice (System) glyph.

Refusals: no faces, no human figures, no DNA helix imagery (cliché), no genealogy-tree imagery (would compete with /familie/stammbaum), no decorative ornamentation, no text in the image.
