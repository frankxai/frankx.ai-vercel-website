⚠️ 2026 Program Consolidation

Microsoft significantly raised revenue thresholds effective February 2026. Direct-Bill partners must reach $1M TTM (trailing twelve-month), distributors $30M per region. Smaller partners should transition to indirect reseller model.

CSP Program Models

Microsoft Cloud Solution Provider program operates in two models: Direct-Bill for large partners with MSP/solutions capabilities, and 2-Tier Cloud Distribution for channel ecosystem builders.

1-Tier Direct-Bill

Partners with managed services or solutions business, able to bill and support at scale.

  • $1M TTM minimum (2026)
  • Bill customers directly
  • Operational assessment required
  • Advanced Support for Partners (ASfP) or Premier Support (PSfP) contract
  • Solutions Partner designation OR 25 Partner Capability Score (PCS) points

2-Tier Cloud Distribution

Distributors who recruit, enable, and grow reseller channels.

  • $30M TTM per authorized region
  • Intentionally high threshold (proven scale/stability)
  • Recruit and enable resellers
  • Channel management infrastructure
  • Multi-region capability

Indirect Resellers

Partners who sell through distributors (recommended for sub-$1M revenue partners).

  • No minimum revenue threshold
  • Sell through authorized distributor
  • Must achieve Solutions Partner OR 25 PCS points to unlock incentives
  • Distributor handles billing/support infrastructure

Solutions Partner Designations

Competency-Based Recognition

Microsoft Solutions Partner designation recognizes expertise across Data & AI, Infrastructure, and Digital & App Innovation. Requires 25 Partner Capability Score (PCS) points minimum.

Data & AI

AI, ML, analytics, databases

Infrastructure

Revenue & Margin Model

Segment-Based Margins

Partner margins vary by customer segment and service type. ISVs can create private offers to extend margins to specific CSP partners.

Co-Sell Incentives

Partners now include Estimated Azure Consumed Revenue in IP Co-Sell referrals. Helps Microsoft assign resources to high-potential cloud deals.

Private Offers

ISVs can create private offers to extend margins to specific CSP partners. Corrected margins and pricing effective February 7, 2026.

Segment Variations

Education and nonprofit typically offer lower or no margins. Commercial/enterprise segments offer standard partner margins.
