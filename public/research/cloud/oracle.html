Partner Tier Structure

Oracle modernized OPN with numerical levels and flexible track selection (Build, Sell, Service). Partners advance based on certifications, customer successes, and Oracle advocacy.

Level 0

Marketplace Publisher only (no membership benefits)

  • Oracle Cloud Marketplace listings
  • Lead generation (BYOL, free listings)
  • No training seats or credits
  • Cannot publish Expertise
Level 1

Entry-level OPN membership with core benefits

  • 5 training seats
  • Dev/test/demo environments
  • Private offers capability
  • Application licenses
  • Can publish Expertise
Level 2

Regional GTM support with partner credits

  • 100 training seats
  • 125K Oracle Partner Credits
  • Regional GTM collaboration
  • Managed GTM campaigns
  • Live virtual classrooms
  • Optional Partner Success Manager
Level 3

Global partner with maximum benefits

  • 500 training seats
  • 750K Oracle Partner Credits
  • Global GTM collaboration
  • Dedicated Partner Success Manager
  • Priority co-selling
  • Volume-based rebates

Revenue Models and Margins

Consumption-Based Partner Rewards

Oracle's enhanced partner program rewards recurring customer engagement rather than one-time transactions. Partners earn ongoing revenue from customer OCI usage, with higher margins for fully managed solutions.

Universal Credits

55% discount off rate card for Level 2+ partners on cash purchases. Credits apply to any OCI service.

AI Workload Margins

30-40% margin over the life of customer contract for OCI AI workloads. Higher margins for managed services.

BYOL Advantage

30-60% cheaper vs. license-included pricing. Partners help customers migrate existing Oracle licenses to OCI.

SaaS Discounts

75% discount on SaaS environments. Remains active through customer renewal cycles.

Partner Credits

Level 2: 125K credits | Level 3: 750K credits. Convert to OCI consumption at discounted rates.

Volume Rebates

Top-performing partners earn additional rebates based on OCI consumption growth. Priority co-selling access.
