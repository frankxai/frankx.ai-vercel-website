Key Insights and Recommendations

Oracle OPN Strengths

Best for partners building recurring revenue models with consumption-based engagement. Lower entry barriers than Microsoft.

  • 55% discount on Universal Credits (Level 2+)
  • 30-40% margin on AI workloads
  • BYOL advantage: 30-60% savings for customers
  • 4 AI Accelerator Packs + 10 OCI AI Blueprints
  • Multi-cloud Universal Credits (Oracle, AWS, Azure, GCP)

AWS APN Strengths

Industry-leading GenAI/Agentic AI funding and strongest MSP program support. Ideal for migration and modernization specialists.

  • $75K total AI MDF available ($25K Agentic + $50K Connect)
  • Partner Greenfield Program (PGP) multi-year co-investment
  • Well-Architected ISV credits: $5K for remediation
  • Enhanced MSP benefits (customer management, strategic services, government)
  • Free evaluation tools and hands-on labs

Microsoft Azure Strengths

Consolidating to large enterprise partners ($1M-$30M thresholds). Best for established partners with Copilot expertise.

  • High barriers eliminate small competitor partners
  • Solutions Partner designation across Data & AI, Infrastructure, App Innovation
  • Expanded Copilot, security, and Azure credits (Feb 2026)
  • Azure Advisor automation for recommendations
  • ISV private offers extend margins

Google Cloud Strengths

Most innovative outcome-based model. Rewards tied to customer retention/ROI metrics rather than pure deal size.

  • 21 competencies (independent of tier): 6 solution, 5 product, 10 industry
  • Outcome-based rebates: retention rates, migration ROI, service quality
  • New Diamond tier ($20M ACV) launching Q1 2026
  • AI-driven program management reduces admin burden
  • Sustainability emphasis in architecture framework
