2026 Partner Tier Structure

Google Cloud overhauled its partner program with a new three-tier structure launching Q1 2026. Tiers based on partner co-sell ACV with six-month transition window for all partners.

Outcome-Based Revenue Model

Variable Rewards Linked to Customer Success

Google Cloud pioneered outcome-based partner rewards in 2026, shifting from flat fees to variable incentives tied to real-world customer metrics. Rewards based on retention rates, ROI from migrations, and long-term engagement.

Retention Rewards

Bonuses tied to customer retention rates. Partners incentivized to ensure customer success, not just initial deal closure.

Migration ROI

Variable rewards linked to measured ROI from customer migrations. Partners rewarded for demonstrable business value.

Co-Sell Influence

Rewards for pre-sales influence, co-innovation, and post-sales support. Recognizes full lifecycle engagement.

Service Delivery Quality

Bonuses for service delivery excellence. Customer satisfaction scores and NPS impact partner rewards.

ISV Innovation

Rewards for ISV partners bringing innovative solutions. Encourages ecosystem platform development.

AI/Automation

Reduced administrative burden via AI-driven program management. Automated rebate calculations and reporting.
