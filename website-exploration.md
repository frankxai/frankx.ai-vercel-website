## Site Architecture Insights

The site is structured as a **comprehensive intelligence hub** with multiple entry points for different audiences. It emphasizes:
- **Conscious AI** practices
- **Systems thinking** approach
- **Multi-stakeholder** engagement (families, creators, enterprises, builders)
- **Field-tested frameworks** rather than theoretical concepts
- **Agent-ready protocols** for AI integration
- **Continuous publishing** cadence (Intelligence Atlas volumes, monthly resources)

The content strategy balances:
- High-level strategic thinking (Intelligence Atlas, roadmaps)
- Practical implementation guides (templates, playbooks, workflows)
- Safety and ethics (family guides, governance frameworks)
- Creative applications (Music Lab, Suno workflows)
- Enterprise consulting (strategy intensives, retainers)
