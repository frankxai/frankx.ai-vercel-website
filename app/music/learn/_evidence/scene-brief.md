# FrankX Music Learning — Canonical Surface Brief

Date: 2026-07-10

## Outcome contract

- Audience: independent adult learners, creator-musicians, and family-supported younger learners.
- Surface: `/music/learn` canonical free-learning hub plus the evidence-critical `/music/learn/science` route.
- First read: this is a free, self-paced map that produces small musical artifacts; it is not a second paid academy.
- Evidence: eight real live learning routes, an exact curriculum sequence, current privacy boundaries, and source-cited research limits.
- Taste: FrankX restraint and technical depth; Ableton-like utility; Vercel/Linear hierarchy; no child-app hero, generic card wall, fake outcomes, or decorative motion.
- Asset tier: C, system-authored exact curriculum UI. No generated or external hero media is required because the live route map is the product proof.

## Composition

1. Hero states the free authority role and gives one primary start action.
2. Proof panel names what is actually live and what data is not collected.
3. Three-step route map: understand, practice, make.
4. Privacy, guardian, and rights boundaries are visible before the final CTA.
5. Science route separates supported observations from frequency/health claims and links primary or authoritative sources.

## Motion score

No non-essential motion. Static hierarchy is the intended reduced-motion and default experience. Hover movement is limited to a 0.5px card lift and arrow translation; content and navigation do not depend on motion.

## Release checks

- Desktop 1440×900 and mobile 390×844 first viewport and route-map inspection.
- Keyboard focus order and visible focus treatment.
- 200% zoom/reflow, reduced-motion, console/runtime, route and outbound-source checks.
- Claim scan, metadata scan, typecheck, lint, production build, security scan, and one Vercel preview.
