# Ana collaboration hub — scene brief

- Surface: intentionally shareable, excluded from search with `noindex, nofollow`.
- Canonical route: `/allies/ana-cancino`; old Ana routes resolve here.
- Audience: Ana, her team, and people Ana or Frank intentionally send the link to.
- First win: understand the eight-stage client workflow and human decision boundaries in under three minutes.
- Core loop: select a stage → inspect ownership → install the shared Codex plugin → rehearse with fictional data.
- Proof object: one keyboard-accessible workflow navigator. No decorative 3D or autoplay motion.
- Mobile: stages stack and remain usable at 320px. Reduced-motion users receive the complete static experience.
- Privacy: no client, candidate, pricing, invoice, private-template, or contact records. Permission for this hub never transfers to a blog, newsletter, social post, or download.
- Security note: `noindex` reduces discovery; it is not authentication. Sensitive material stays in approved private systems.
