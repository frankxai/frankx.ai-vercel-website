Last week friends asked what my AI stack could do for their startup.

The answer: a complete operating system, installed in one day.

Not a chatbot setup. A repository that teaches the AI who they are:
→ a doctrine file every session reads automatically
→ a design contract their brand can't drift from
→ a claims gate that blocks regulated marketing language before it ships
→ a business memory that makes week 30 smarter than week 1

The install service around it is the FrankX Foundry. Application-only, a few installs per quarter, priority for sustainable, healthcare, and meaningful businesses.
