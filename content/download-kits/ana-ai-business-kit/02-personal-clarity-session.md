# 02 - Personal Clarity Session

Purpose: give Ana a calm, repeatable structure for 1:1 work.

## Positioning

A personal clarity session is a focused conversation for someone who wants to name what is happening, understand the pattern, and leave with a few grounded next steps.

## Intake

Ask before the session:

- What is the main question you want to bring?
- What have you already tried?
- What feels most confusing right now?
- What would feel lighter or clearer after our session?
- Is there anything I should avoid or handle with extra care?

## Session flow

1. Welcome and boundary.
2. Let the client tell the story.
3. Reflect back the main thread.
4. Ask clarifying questions.
5. Name the pattern, tension, or decision point.
6. Choose 1 to 3 next steps.
7. Close with grounding and consent for follow-up.

## AI backstage use

Ana can paste anonymized notes into a private AI workspace and ask:

> Turn these anonymized notes into a warm follow-up letter. Keep the language simple. Do not diagnose. Do not invent details. Include 3 grounded next steps and 3 reflection prompts.

## Boundaries

- Do not present this as therapy unless Ana is legally qualified and chooses that frame.
- Do not diagnose.
- Do not use client content in public without written approval.
- Do not let AI write as Ana without Ana editing the final letter.

## Deliverable

Send one short letter within 48 hours:

- What I heard.
- What seems important.
- What you may try next.
- Questions to sit with.
- Optional next container.
