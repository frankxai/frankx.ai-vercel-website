# Privacy Boundaries

This kit is designed for public-safe business building.

## Never publish without approval

- private client stories
- screenshots of conversations
- session notes
- names, employers, or identifying details
- private faith, family, health, or relationship material
- testimonials
- direct quotes
- claims about Ana's credentials that have not been verified

## AI rules

- Prefer anonymized notes.
- Keep sensitive client material out of public tools.
- Do not ask AI to diagnose.
- Do not ask AI to imitate Ana in final public voice without review.
- Do not let AI invent credentials, client outcomes, or quotes.

## Public-safe content

Usually safe:

- general themes
- approved research notes
- public website language
- offer descriptions
- anonymized patterns with no identifying details
- prompts and exercises created by Ana

## Review question

Before publishing, ask:

Would the person involved feel respected, protected, and accurately represented if they saw this?

If the answer is not clearly yes, keep it private.
