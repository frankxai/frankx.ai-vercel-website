# 06 - Freedom Engine Roadmap

Purpose: help Ana build a business that can support travel, spacious work, and recurring income without losing the human core.

## Principle

Freedom comes from simple offers, repeatable delivery, careful follow-up, public trust, and small digital products. AI helps with the system, not the soul of the work.

## 0 to 30 days

- Choose one core offer.
- Create intake questions.
- Create a follow-up letter template.
- Run 3 to 5 sessions or one small circle.
- Collect private feedback.

## 31 to 90 days

- Publish 2 useful posts per week from approved themes.
- Package one circle.
- Create one workshop outline.
- Build a simple email list.
- Track repeated questions and outcomes.

## 3 to 6 months

- Run the workshop twice.
- Create a small paid digital product.
- Build a content library.
- Test one team-facing offer.
- Create a monthly rhythm for sessions, circles, and content.

## 6 to 12 months

- Sell a seasonal circle.
- Sell a workshop package.
- Publish a paid guide or card deck.
- Create travel weeks and delivery weeks.
- Add ACOS or another agent workspace only if it saves time.

## Metrics

Track:

- leads
- booked calls
- paid sessions
- circle seats
- workshop inquiries
- email subscribers
- repeat themes
- testimonials approved for public use
- energy cost for Ana

If an offer makes money but drains Ana, redesign it.
