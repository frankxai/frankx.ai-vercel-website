# 01 - Offer Map

Goal: help Ana sell simple, honest offers before building a larger platform.

## Core public language

Ana helps people create clearer inner language, better choices, and more humane ways of working with themselves and others.

The FrankX system helps Ana turn that work into repeatable offers, client follow-up, content, and digital products.

## Existing baseline offers

### Personal Clarity Session

Best for: one person who wants to understand a current life, work, relationship, or inner transition.

Output:
- A focused conversation.
- A short written reflection letter.
- 1 to 3 next actions.
- Optional follow-up prompts.

### Guided Reflection Circle

Best for: a small group that wants shared reflection, meaning-making, and a safe container.

Output:
- A theme.
- A live group session.
- Shared prompts.
- A closing letter or recap.

### Self-Knowledge Workshop

Best for: a group that wants a structured experience around self-knowledge, identity, values, or direction.

Output:
- Workshop theme.
- Exercises.
- Reflection prompts.
- Take-home material.

## Proposed expansion

### HR and Soul Work for Teams

This is proposed, not final.

Best for: founders, teams, and people leaders who want more humane hiring, role clarity, trust, and communication.

Output:
- Team clarity session.
- Hiring or role reflection map.
- Communication patterns.
- Follow-up letter with next steps.

## Pricing ladder draft

Use this as a thinking tool, not final pricing.

- Entry: digital guide, reflection cards, or recorded workshop.
- Core: personal clarity session.
- Group: reflection circle.
- Premium: self-knowledge workshop.
- Business: team clarity workshop.
- Retainer: monthly content, people, or culture advisory.

## Decide next

Answer these before selling:

1. Which offer feels easiest for Ana to deliver with care this month?
2. What outcome can the client understand in one sentence?
3. What is included, and what is not included?
4. What private material must never be used for marketing?
5. What does a beautiful follow-up look like?
