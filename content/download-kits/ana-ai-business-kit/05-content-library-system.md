# 05 - Content Library System

Purpose: help Ana publish consistently without exposing private client material.

## Library buckets

Create folders or database fields for:

- questions people ask
- themes Ana is researching
- books and notes
- session patterns, anonymized
- circle prompts
- workshop exercises
- personal reflections approved for public use
- product ideas

## Weekly workflow

1. Capture raw notes.
2. Mark privacy level.
3. Choose one theme.
4. Draft three posts.
5. Turn the best post into a newsletter, guide, or workshop asset.
6. Save client-sensitive material in private only.

## AI prompt

> Review these approved public notes. Suggest 5 simple content ideas for Ana. Keep the tone calm, precise, and human. Do not invent personal stories. Do not make therapy, medical, or spiritual authority claims.

## Product path

Good content can become:

- reflection cards
- paid guide
- workshop worksheet
- short course
- circle theme
- team workshop
