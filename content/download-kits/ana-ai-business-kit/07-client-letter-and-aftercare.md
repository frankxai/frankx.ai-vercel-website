# 07 - Client Letter And Aftercare

Purpose: make follow-up feel beautiful, useful, and repeatable.

## Letter structure

Subject: Notes from our session

Hi [name],

Thank you for trusting me with the conversation today.

What I heard:

- [thread 1]
- [thread 2]
- [thread 3]

What seems important:

- [pattern or tension]
- [value or need]
- [next choice]

Next steps:

1. [small action]
2. [small action]
3. [small action]

Questions to sit with:

- [question]
- [question]
- [question]

With care,  
Ana

## Aftercare options

- Send prompts.
- Send a reading path.
- Invite to a circle.
- Invite to a workshop.
- Book a follow-up session.

## AI prompt

> Draft a follow-up letter from anonymized notes. Keep the voice warm, direct, and simple. Do not diagnose. Do not invent facts. Do not make claims about outcomes. Include what was heard, what seems important, next steps, and reflection questions.

## Quality check

Before sending:

- Does it sound like Ana?
- Is anything too intimate to write down?
- Is any claim too strong?
- Are the next steps small enough?
- Is the client invited without pressure?
