# 03 - Guided Reflection Circle

Purpose: help Ana host small groups without turning the experience into a complicated production.

## Best use

Use this when a group wants to explore one theme together:

- transition
- identity
- work and meaning
- inner dialogue
- faith and uncertainty
- relationships
- purpose and next chapter

## Structure

Length: 75 to 120 minutes  
Group size: 4 to 12 people

## Flow

1. Arrival and simple grounding.
2. Name the theme.
3. Agreement: confidentiality, kindness, no fixing.
4. First prompt: what is alive for you around this theme?
5. Second prompt: what feels true but hard to say?
6. Pair or silent reflection.
7. Group sharing.
8. Close with one sentence: what am I taking with me?

## Materials

- Theme note.
- 5 to 7 reflection prompts.
- Optional reading or quote, only if Ana approves it.
- Follow-up letter template.

## AI backstage use

Use AI to:

- organize a theme brief
- draft prompt options
- summarize anonymized themes after the circle
- turn approved recurring insights into future content ideas

Do not paste names, private stories, or sensitive details into AI tools unless the workspace is private and consent is clear.

## Follow-up

Send the group:

- a thank you
- the prompts
- a short reflection letter
- one next invitation
