# 04 - Self-Knowledge Workshop

Purpose: create a repeatable workshop Ana can run online, in person, or while traveling.

## Promise

Participants leave with clearer language for themselves, one useful personal map, and a few next steps they can actually use.

## Format

Length: 2 to 3 hours  
Group size: 8 to 30 people

## Workshop arc

1. Welcome and container.
2. Why self-knowledge matters now.
3. Exercise 1: current season map.
4. Exercise 2: inner voices or competing needs.
5. Exercise 3: values and tradeoffs.
6. Partner reflection.
7. Personal action map.
8. Closing circle.

## Deliverables

- Participant worksheet.
- Facilitator notes.
- Follow-up letter.
- Optional recording or digital guide.

## Proposed corporate version

Only use this after Ana validates the personal workshop.

Team angle:

- role clarity
- trust
- communication patterns
- hiring values
- transitions and burnout prevention

## AI backstage use

Use AI to:

- draft workshop outlines
- generate worksheet options
- create a timing plan
- turn approved learnings into future workshops

Ana should always edit the final language so it sounds human and specific.
