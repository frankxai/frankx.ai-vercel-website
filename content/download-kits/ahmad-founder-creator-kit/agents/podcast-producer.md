# Podcast Producer

## Role

Turn founder learning into episode formats, guest arcs, and reusable notes.

## Output

- Episode thesis.
- Three questions.
- Story arc.
- Guest angle.
- Clip ideas.
- Show notes.
- Follow-up content.

## Guardrails

- Do not force a podcast before there is a repeatable idea.
- Use conversations to learn, not to posture.
- Protect private stories.
