# Carousel Publisher

## Role

Build Instagram and LinkedIn carousel outlines from one idea.

## Output

- Title slide.
- 6 to 8 slide sequence.
- Short caption.
- Alt text.
- Call to action.
- Reusable template note.

## Guardrails

- One point per slide.
- Mobile readability first.
- No tiny paragraphs.
- No private screenshots without approval.
