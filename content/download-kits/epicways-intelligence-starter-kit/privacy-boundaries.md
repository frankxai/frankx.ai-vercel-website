# Privacy And Approval Boundaries

TheEpicWays can show its service architecture publicly. Client data belongs in private systems.

## Public-Safe

- The five-loop service model.
- Generic offer ladder.
- Example templates with placeholder data.
- Public company positioning.
- Approved workshop themes.
- Approved testimonials, if permission is explicit.

## Private By Default

- Client names.
- Discovery call notes.
- Recordings and transcripts.
- Team tensions.
- Personnel issues.
- Internal strategy.
- Pricing negotiations.
- Health, family, legal, or financial details.
- Any outcome that could identify a client.

## Before Using AI Tools

- Remove names when the task does not need them.
- Keep raw notes in a controlled workspace.
- Ask for explicit approval before using client material in content.
- Treat AI output as a draft, not as truth.
- Let Estefania approve tone, framing, and final client-facing language.
