## Content Rules

- Do not reveal client names without written approval.
- Do not reveal internal tensions, financial details, personnel issues, or private outcomes.
- Use patterns, not gossip.
- Keep the voice human, clear, and direct.
- Make the idea useful to leaders, not self-congratulatory.
