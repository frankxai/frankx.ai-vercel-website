## Prompt Bank

- What are we avoiding because it feels expensive to say?
- Where are we confusing activity with progress?
- What would change if ownership was clearer?
- What does this team need more of: clarity, courage, rhythm, or trust?
- What sentence would make the next week easier?

## Energy Checkpoints

- Start - are people present or still arriving mentally?
- Middle - is the room becoming honest or polite?
- Decision point - are commitments specific enough?
- Close - does the room know what happens next?
