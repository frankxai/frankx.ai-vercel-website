## Discovery Questions

Ask only what will help design a better room.

1. What would make this session clearly worth it one week later?
2. What conversations are not happening right now?
3. Where do teams lose energy or clarity?
4. What should people leave ready to do differently?
5. What topics are sensitive or off-limits?
6. Who must feel seen for the session to work?
7. What language does this team already use?
8. What has been tried before?
