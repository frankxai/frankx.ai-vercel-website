# 02 - Offer Architect

Use this to turn the signal brief into a clear service path.

## Offer Ladder

| Offer | Best For | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Leadership Diagnostic | Client needs clarity before choosing a format. | Brief, tension map, recommended path. |
| Intensive Workshop | Client needs one focused room. | Agenda, exercises, action plan, follow-up pack. |
| Leadership Lab | Client needs repeated behavior change. | Multi-session program, manager tools, progress rhythm. |
| EpicVoices Event | Client needs a high-energy public or private experience. | Interview, panel, curated room, community conversation. |
| Client Intelligence Retainer | Client needs ongoing preparation and follow-up. | Monthly brief, room design, memory, dashboard, content support. |

## Fit Check

- What is the client trying to change?
- Is this a one-room problem or a repeated-behavior problem?
- Does the client need diagnosis, facilitation, training, or momentum?
- What must be delivered after the room?
- What can TheEpicWays own better than anyone else?

## Proposal Outline

1. Context - what we heard.
2. Opportunity - what can change.
3. TheEpicWays approach - how Estefania will lead the room.
4. Format - structure, timing, and audience.
5. Deliverables - before, during, after.
6. Investment - fee, payment terms, optional add-ons.
7. Next step - one clear action.

## Outcome Language

Use plain language the client can repeat internally.

- People leave with cleaner priorities.
- Managers leave with better scripts.
- Teams leave with shared language.
- Leaders leave with more courage for direct conversations.
- The organization leaves with next actions, owners, and a follow-up rhythm.
