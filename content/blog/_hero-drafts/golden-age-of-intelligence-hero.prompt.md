## FULL STRUCTURED PROMPT (for nb-generate or image_gen)

## CONCEPT
A majestic Da Vinci-inspired intelligence orrery representing the Golden Age of Intelligence — the moment when AI tools, zero-marginal-cost distribution, and sovereign creator systems converge to make exponential reach possible for individuals. The image captures the "12-day accelerator" spirit as orbiting ritual stations around a radiant core, with living organic connections showing compounding growth.

## ART DIRECTION
Dark Premium Glass with Da Vinci Dark (C2) sub-variant: deep navy background (#0f172a to #0a0e1a), ornate golden ratio frames and compass rose accents, parchment-like subtle textures on floating panels, hand-drawn technical line quality merged with premium 3D glass and chrome. Professional architectural visualization meets Renaissance codex. 16:9 widescreen hero. High information density but scannable at blog thumbnail size. Every element purposeful.

## ORGANIZING METAPHOR
A grand central golden sun (the "Intelligence Core") surrounded by a clockwork armillary sphere / orrery whose 12 elliptical orbits hold distinct ritual stations. Living bioluminescent root networks and canopy vines rise from a fertile base "Creator Earth" and thread through the entire mechanism, feeding energy upward and distributing glowing "reach orbs" outward to the edges of the frame. Technology as nervous system of a living tree of knowledge.

## STYLE
dark-premium-glass:davinci-dark

## VIEWPOINT
Dramatic low three-quarter angle, looking slightly upward at the central sun and the sweeping arcs of the orrery, creating heroic scale and depth. 30 degree tilt, cinematic.

## ORGANIC ANCHOR
Thick bioluminescent root networks (soft #10B981 pulse) rising from the bottom "earth" layer, wrapping around the base of the orrery arms and threading into the stations like living circuitry. Tiny crystal flowers and leaf buds bloom where roots meet metal/glass. The vines visibly carry golden light from the core outward. Fur-like moss texture on the earth base. Never sterile — the machine is clearly being grown and nourished by life.

## FOREGROUND
Slightly out-of-focus crystalline root fragments, unfurled scroll fragments with tiny Latin + code annotations ("Scientia × Leverage"), and a few loose glowing "reach seeds" (small orbs) drifting toward viewer. Creates intimate entry point and depth.

## MIDGROUND
The full armillary orrery with the 12 labeled stations in motion arcs around the blazing central Intelligence Core. The vines visibly connect everything. The "Daily Ship" and "Amplifier" stations are crispest.

## BACKGROUND
Deep navy (#0f172a) cosmic void with faint constellation dot grid (gold and cyan dots), very subtle nebula wisps in purple, and faint outward-streaming light rays from the core suggesting exponential spread. Distant tiny creator figures or workshops at the horizon of the frame for scale.

## ATMOSPHERE
Warm directional key light from the central core (upper-leftish) casting soft long shadows and rim light on the glass elements. Gentle volumetric god rays through the vines. Floating golden dust motes and pollen-like particles in the beams. Soft subsurface glow on the organic elements. Overall mood of earned optimism, sovereign power, and living intelligence — not cold tech, not woo.

## EMOTIONAL TONE
Awe at the scale of what one creator can now compound, quiet confidence, invitation to step into the system, warmth from the organic life within the grand mechanism. Feels like standing inside a cathedral that is also a growing tree that is also a precise observatory.

## NEGATIVE
No chrome-robot sterility, no pastel, no claymorphic, no cartoon, no flat icons, no single-color wash, no blurry or tiny unreadable text, no generic "futuristic AI" floating nodes without metaphor, no stock photo people, no watermarks, no emoji, no oversaturated, no empty negative space that feels sparse, no outdated model names or specific logos, no self-help guru gradients. All labels are crisp and purposeful. The organic must be unmistakably alive before any circuit is admired.
