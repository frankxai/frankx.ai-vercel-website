## The one-page protocol
- Daily: 5-min examen (Part 1) + slow read (Part 2) + 1 sit (Part 3).
- Weekly: one real human conversation. One act of service. One review.
- The AI's job: keep the appointments, answer the questions, get out of the way.

## Designing it to scale without getting weird
- No guru (including me). No oracle. No streak-shame.
- Sovereignty: your practice, your data, your tradition. Portable, not platform-locked.
- Community is human-first; AI is the scaffolding, never the congregation.
- The anti-funnel principle: this can't become a content trap. Why that matters here more than anywhere.

## The million-person math (honestly)
- Not a launch metric. A picture: a million quiet protocols changing a million rooms.
- What that would actually require, and what it would NOT require (me being a guru).
