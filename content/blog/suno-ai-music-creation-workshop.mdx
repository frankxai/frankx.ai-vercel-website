What if you could turn this thought:

## What You'll Learn

The [Suno AI Music Creation Workshop](/workshops/suno-music-creation) teaches:

### The 5-Layer Prompt Architecture

Every great Suno prompt contains five layers:

| Layer | Purpose | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| **Foundation** | Genre and style | "Ambient electronic meditation" |
| **Mood** | Emotional landscape | "Ethereal and transcendent" |
| **Instrumentation** | Sonic palette | "Crystal bowls, synth pads" |
| **Production** | Mix quality | "Spacious cathedral reverb" |
| **Intention** | Energetic purpose | "Deep inner peace" |

### Four Signature Styles

**1. Conscious Electronic** (60-90 BPM)
Perfect for meditation, yoga, and breathwork.

**2. Epic Motivation** (120-140 BPM)
For workouts, presentations, and launches.

**3. Creative Flow** (80-110 BPM)
Background for deep work, writing, and coding.

**4. Frequency Healing** (under 60 BPM)
Sleep, transformation, and cellular healing.

### Frequency Science

Learn to incorporate:
- **528Hz** - The "love frequency" for DNA repair
- **432Hz** - Natural cosmic tuning
- **Binaural beats** - For specific brain states

## Example: Creating a Meditation Track

Here's the complete prompt structure:

```
Ambient electronic meditation, ethereal and transcendent,
with crystal singing bowls tuned to 528Hz, soft synth pads,
binaural theta waves, gentle wind chimes, spacious cathedral
reverb, pristine production, intention: deep inner peace
and heart healing, slow and drifting at 60 BPM.
```

This prompt hits all five layers and creates a professional-quality meditation track.
