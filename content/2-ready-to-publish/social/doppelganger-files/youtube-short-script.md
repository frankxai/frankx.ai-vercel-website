Title: I Built Five Future Selves and Gave Them Jobs

Duration: 45-60 seconds

## Shot List

1. Face camera: "I built five future selves and made them run my brands."
2. Cut to operator board.
3. Screen/text overlay: "Future self -> operator."
4. Show five cards: FrankX, Arcanea, Starlight, GenCreator, Reality Architect.
5. Show today's selector.
6. Show one proof artifact.
7. CTA screen.

## Script

I built five future selves and made them run my brands.

Not as visualization.

As operators.

FrankX writes the creator systems.

Arcanea builds the fantasy worlds.

Starlight proves the agentic OS works.

GenCreator turns one idea into 20 assets.

Reality Architect asks:

Where is the evidence?

That is the difference.

Future self is vague until it has a job, a KPI, a proof artifact, an audience, and a capture point.

So every morning I choose:

Which operator is running today?
What artifact proves it?
Who is it for?
Where do they go next?

That is the Doppelganger Files.

## Caption

Do not visualize the future self. Give him a job and make him ship proof.
