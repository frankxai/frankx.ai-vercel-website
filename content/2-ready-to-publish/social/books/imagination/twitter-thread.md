## Tweet 2 (Context)
There is a critical distinction most people miss:

Fantasy is imagination without structure. It drifts. It entertains. It produces zero outcomes.

Imagination is directed. It serves a purpose. It holds a model of something that does not yet exist long enough to examine, test, and build it.

## Tweet 3 (Insight - Inner Theater)
Your brain does not fully distinguish between a vividly imagined experience and a lived one.

People who vividly imagined playing piano activated the same motor cortex regions as people who physically played. Imagination is not entertainment. It is a simulation engine.

## Tweet 4 (Insight - Tesla)
Tesla ran entire machines in his imagination for weeks, then disassembled them mentally to check for wear on imaginary components.

When he finally built physical prototypes, they worked exactly as his mental models predicted. His AC motor design from 1888 is still used today.

## Tweet 5 (Insight - Mental Models)
Your mental models are the operating system of your imagination.

First Principles strips assumptions to bedrock. Inversion asks "How do I fail?" then avoids those things. Second-Order Thinking asks "And then what?" two moves ahead.

The quality of your models determines the quality of your thinking.
