## What we built

The first VibeClubs surface: a landing page that explains the membership thesis, captures interest, and routes to Stripe for the founding-member tier. Behind it, a minimal member dashboard that reads from the Starlight registry.

## The stack and why

- **Next.js 16** — landing and dashboard share the same App Router tree.
- **Stripe** — checkout for the founding tier; subscription billing for monthly.
- **Resend** — onboarding sequence; routes through the same `/api/subscribe` endpoint as the rest of frankx.ai.

## What worked

- Reusing the `EmailSignup` component with a new `listType` was a one-line change.

## What broke

- Pricing posture took 3 iterations to land. Founding tier ended up below the workshop price, sprint price, and template pack — a clear ladder.
